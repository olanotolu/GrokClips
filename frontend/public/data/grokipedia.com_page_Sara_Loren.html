Sara Loren

Mona Lizza Hussain (Urdu: ساره لورين), better known as Sara Loren (born 11 December 1985), is a Pakistani actress and model, known for working in Hindi as well as Urdu movies and television. She gained prominence with her role as Nisha in the 2013 Bollywood film Murder 3.[2] After a hiatus, she returned in 2022 with the Pakistani film Ishrat Made in China and continues to appear in cross-border projects, including the upcoming 2025 Punjabi film Bullah.[3][]

Early life

Loren was born on 11 December 1985 in Kuwait City, in a Rajput family.[4][5] Her grandfather migrated to Kuwait from Rajasthan following the Partition of India, her father was from Rajasthan, and her mother was born in Pakistan.[4][5] During an interview in India she said she belongs to a multi-cultural background as her father is a Rajput from Rajasthan, and also said that she goes to the temple.[4] As a teenager, she relocated with her family from Kuwait to Lahore, Pakistan, following the death of her father.[6][5]

Career

Loren made her acting debut in 2003 with the television serial Rabia Zinda Rahegi. She has also appeared in Mahnoor, Meharun Nisa, Makan, Mehar Bano aur Shah Bano, Sandal, Riyasat, Help of a Ghost, Dupatta, Umrao Jaan Ada, Madhosh, and was nominated for Best Drama Actress for her performance in Main Mar Gai Shaukat Ali at the 2nd Pakistan Media Awards.[7] She made her screen debut with Pooja Bhatt's Bollywood romantic thriller Kajraare (2010), opposite Himesh Reshammiya.[8] In 2011, she had a cameo appearance in the song "Love Mein Ghum" in Reema Khan's Love Mein Ghum.[9] The following year, she played the seductress Nisha in Murder 3 (2013).[10] That same year, she made her Lollywood debut starring as Anjum Hayat Khan in Yasir Nawaz's Anjuman (2013), for which she won the Tarang Housefull Awards for Best Actress in a Leading Role.[11] In 2014, she performed the item number "Saiyaan" in Syed Faisal Bukhari's Sultanat.) She then starred as Barkhaa, a small-town girl who becomes a bar dancer, in Shadaab Mirza's Barkhaa (2015). The film ran for 50 days but was a commercial disaster.[12] After a brief hiatus, Loren completed Ishq Click (2016), playing a supermodel, though it received limited release.[13] She made a cameo in Jawani Phir Nahi Ani 2 (2018) and portrayed Payal in Fraud Saiyyan (2019). In 2022, she starred as Jia, a Chinese martial artist, in the Pakistani film Ishrat Made in China.[14] Her short film Consent was released in 2024.[15] As of 2025, she is set to star opposite Shaan Shahid in the Punjabi action film Bullah.[16]
