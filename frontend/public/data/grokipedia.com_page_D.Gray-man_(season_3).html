D.Gray-man season 3

The third season of the D.Gray-man anime series, called the "2nd stage", was directed by Osamu Nabeshima and produced by TMS Entertainment. It adapts Katsura Hoshino's manga. Like the rest of the series, it follows the adventures of Allen Walker, an Exorcist that wields the power of "Innocence" to fight against the Earl of Millennium, an ancient sorcerer seeking to destroy the world with monsters called akuma. The season ran from October 2, 2007, to April 1, 2008, on TV Tokyo in Japan. The first seven DVD compilations of the "2nd stage," each containing four episodes (with the seventh covering episodes 76â€“79, but only up to 77 for this season), were released by Aniplex between March 5, 2008, and September 3, 2008. In June 2016, the series was licensed by Funimation for an English-language release in North America. Funimation released Season Three, Part One (episodes 52â€“64) on DVD on October 3, 2017, and Part Two (episodes 65â€“77) on January 2, 2018. Four pieces of theme music are used for the season: two opening themes and three ending themes. The opening theme for episodes 52â€“76 is "Doubt & Trust" by Access; for episode 77, it is "GekidÅ" by UVERworld.
