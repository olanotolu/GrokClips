List of current equipment of the Iraqi Ground Forces

The following is a list of equipment currently in use with the Iraqi Ground Forces as of 2024. For a list of previous equipment, please see List of former equipment of the Iraqi Ground Forces. The Ground Forces have undergone significant modernization since 2014, with ongoing acquisitions including a potential 2025 deal for 250 K2 Black Panther main battle tanks.[1]
