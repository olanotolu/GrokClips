D.R.E.A.M. The Tour

JoJo Siwa D.R.E.A.M. The Tour or D.R.E.A.M. The Tour was the debut concert tour of American singer and dancer JoJo Siwa, supported by AEG Presents, which ran from May 17, 2019, to March 12, 2022.[1] Meant to support Siwa's debut EPs D.R.E.A.M. The Music (2018) and Celebrate (2019), the tour was initially announced in November 2018 with North American dates released at the same time; dates for Australia and the U.K. were announced later.[2] Girl pop group XOMG POP served as the opening act.

JoJo Siwa D.R.E.A.M. Tour

Background

On November 7, 2018, Siwa first announced a 26-show tour performing in theaters in North America.[2] On December 11, 2018, Siwa extended the North American leg, adding 28 shows in arenas, amphitheaters, and theaters.[3] On April 4, 2019, 17 dates were added.[4] On June 10, 2019, 11 more dates were added, marking the total number of shows to 82 in the first leg.[5] On June 18, 2019, dates were announced for Europe in the United Kingdom and Ireland.[6] On September 10, 2019, Siwa announced that the tour will stop in Oceania.[7] On November 18, 2019, she added 50 shows touring North America a second time.[8] In 2020, due to the COVID-19 pandemic, many tour dates were postponed and rescheduled to 2021 and 2022, enabling the tour to resume and conclude on March 12, 2022.[9]
