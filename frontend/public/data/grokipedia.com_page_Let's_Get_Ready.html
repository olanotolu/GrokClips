Let's Get Ready

Let's Get Ready is the fourth studio album by American rapper Mystikal, released on September 26, 2000, by Jive Records. It serves as Mystikal's first album since parting ways with No Limit Records. The album features appearances from Pharrell Williams, Da Brat, Nivea, and Petey Pablo. The album debuted at number 1 on the Billboard 200, selling 330,663 copies in its first week.[1] It was certified double platinum by the RIAA in the United States.[2] The album featured Mystikal's two biggest singles, "Shake Ya Ass", which peaked at number 13 on the Billboard Hot 100, and "Danger (Been So Long)", which peaked at number 14.
