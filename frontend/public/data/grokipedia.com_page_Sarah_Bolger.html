Sarah Bolger

Sarah Lee Bolger (born 28 February 1991)[1][2][3] is an Irish actress. She starred in the films In America (2003), Stormbreaker (2006), The Spiderwick Chronicles (2008), The Moth Diaries (2011), The Lazarus Effect (2015), Emelie (2015), and A Good Woman Is Hard to Find (2019), Things Will Be Different (2024), Descendent (2025)[4], and The Up and Comer (2025). On television, she portrayed Princess Mary Tudor in The Tudors (2008–2010), for which she won an IFTA award, and Princess Aurora in Once Upon a Time (2012–2015). Bolger also appeared on the series Into the Badlands (2015–2017) and starred on Mayans M.C. (2018–2023) and the miniseries Rematch (2024).

Early life and education

Bolger was born in Dublin to father Derek, a butcher and mother Monica, a housewife. She grew up in the Southside suburb of Rathfarnham.[1][5] Her younger sister, Emma, also acted and has since pursued academia.[6] Bolger attended Loreto High School Beaufort from 2003 to 2009 and took classes at Ann Kavanagh's Young People's Theatre.[7][8]

Career

Bolger starred in In America with her sister, Emma. From 2008 to 2010, she portrayed Princess Mary Tudor in The Tudors.[1] Bolger starred in Stormbreaker alongside Alex Pettyfer. She also starred in the film adaptation of the children's novel The Spiderwick Chronicles. She filmed a pilot called Locke & Key.[9] Bolger guest starred as Princess Aurora in the second, third, and fourth seasons of the fairy tale drama, Once Upon a Time.[10] Bolger played the female lead role, Umi, in the English version of the 2011 Studio Ghibli film From Up on Poppy Hill, and starred in the supernatural thriller The Moth Diaries (2011).[11] She starred as Lucy in As Cool as I Am in 2013. In 2014, she made an appearance on the TV series, Mixology.[12] Bolger co-starred in David Gelb's thriller film The Lazarus Effect.[13] In 2015, she starred from 2015 to 2017 in the AMC martial arts series Into the Badlands and the Freddie Steinmark biopic My All American, and as the title character in the horror film Emelie. In March 2017, it was reported by Deadline Hollywood that Bolger was to star in the crime-thriller A Good Woman Is Hard to Find directed by Abner Pastoll.[14] Filming completed in December 2017,[15] with the film released in 2019.[16][17] From 2018 to 2023, Bolger starred as Emily Galindo in the FX series Mayans M.C..[18] In 2025, she starred in the horror thriller Descendent[4] and the legal thriller The Up and Comer.[19]

Other projects

In January 2011, Bolger was selected to be in photographer Kevin Abosch's project "The Face of Ireland"[20] alongside other Irish celebrities including Sinéad O'Connor, Neil Jordan, and Pierce Brosnan.[21]
