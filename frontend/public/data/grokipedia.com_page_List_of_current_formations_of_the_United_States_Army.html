List of current formations of the United States Army

This is a list of current formations of the United States Army as of October 2025, which is constantly changing as the Army changes its structure over time, including under the ongoing Army Transformation Initiative that converts Infantry Brigade Combat Teams to Mobile Brigade Combat Teams to enhance mobility and lethality.[1] Due to the nature of those changes, specifically the restructuring of brigades into autonomous modular brigades, debate has arisen as to whether brigades are units or formations; for the purposes of this list, higher-level formations such as armies, corps, and divisions are the primary focus, with independent brigades listed separately. Active status formations are shown in bold.
