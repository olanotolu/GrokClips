Sarah Schechter (producer)

Sarah Schechter (born 1976) is an American television and film producer. Schechter is chairperson and partner at Berlanti Productions, and the co-founder of Berlanti-Schechter Films. In October 2020, with 17 scripted series then on the air, she became one of the most prolific producers in television history.[1]

Early life and education

Schechter was born in Boston and grew up in Massachusetts before moving to New York City at the age of nine.[2] Her parents divorced when she was two years old; her mother was involved in efforts to legalize abortion. Her father, Danny Schechter, a grandson of Russian-Jewish immigrant socialists,[3] was a human rights activist, author, filmmaker and television producer.[4] She attended the preparatory school Milton Academy, where she was valedictorian,[5] and in 1998 graduated from UC Santa Cruz with a degree in film theory.[6] [7]

Career

Schechter began her career in New York, working for the documentarian Barbara Kopple and on independent film projects. She later moved to Los Angeles, where she spent six years working for the producer Barry Mendel, and nine years as a development executive at Warner Bros. Pictures. Schechter began working with Greg Berlanti on Life as We Know It, and in 2014 was named president of Berlanti Productions. In February 2020, she was promoted to partner and chairperson, coinciding with the formation of Berlanti-Schechter Films.[1][8] As of October 2020, Schechter had 17 scripted series on the air, and she has produced films including My Policeman, Unpregnant, Free Guy, Alice & Freda Forever, and _Red, White & Royal Blue.[1][9] In 2023, Berlanti Productions extended its overall deal with Warner Bros. Television Group through 2027, with more than 15 shows on air or in production.[10]
