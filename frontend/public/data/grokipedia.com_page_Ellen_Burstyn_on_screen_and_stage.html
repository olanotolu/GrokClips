Ellen Burstyn on screen and stage

Ellen Burstyn is an American actress of stage and screen. She has also had a distinguished career in theater. She is known for her film performances in Peter Bogdanovich's The Last Picture Show (1971), William Friedkin's The Exorcist (1973) and its sequel The Exorcist: Believer (2023), Martin Scorsese's Alice Doesn't Live Here Anymore (1974), and Requiem for a Dream (2000). She also has appeared in such films as Harry and Tonto (1974), Same Time, Next Year (1978), Resurrection (1980), Pieces of a Woman (2020), and Queen Bees (2021). She is also known for her performances in television such as Big Love (2007â€“2011), Political Animals (2012), Louie (2014), House of Cards (2016), and The First Lady (2022).[1]
