Léo Stronda

Leonardo Schulz Cardoso (born July 5, 1992), known professionally as Léo Stronda, is a Brazilian bodybuilder, YouTuber, and former rapper. He was the vocalist and a co-founder of the hip hop duo Bonde da Stronda. In 2023, following his conversion to Protestant Christianity, he announced his departure from the group. He is also known for his clothing line, XXT Corporation. Stronda co-created the Brazilian bodybuilding YouTube channel "Fábrica de Monstros", which he left in 2018 due to ownership disputes with partners. His personal channel, "LEO STRONDA", has approximately 3.6 million subscribers as of October 2025.

Leonardo Schulz Cardoso

Biography

Leonardo Schulz Cardoso was born 5 July 1992, in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. He is a childhood friend of Diego Villanueva, with whom he formed the group Bonde da Stronda in 2006. Cardoso is also a bodybuilder who began to be interested in the sport at the age of 15. The single "Bonde da Maromba" references his bodybuilding and was released along with a music video on 17 December 2012.[1] His social media presence includes posts on nutrition, health, wellness, training tips as well as projects and competitions. He remained active with Bonde da Stronda until 2023, when he left the group to focus on other pursuits.[2]

Clothing line

Léo Stronda is the founder and owner of the clothing line XXT Corporation, which specializes in streetwear and casual apparel.[3] The brand remains active as of 2025, offering products such as t-shirts, jackets, and accessories through its online store.[4]

Personal life

On March 6, 2023, Stronda announced on Instagram that he is no longer part of Bonde da Stronda, citing his recent conversion to evangelical Christianity and stating that he now defends another doctrine.[5] In 2023, Stronda converted to evangelical Christianity. He continues to promote his faith publicly as of 2025.[6]

Viral incident (2017)

On January 12, 2017, an explicit nude photograph of Léo Stronda was leaked online, showing him taking a mirror selfie in which his flaccid penis was visible. The image quickly spread across Brazilian social media platforms, including Twitter, WhatsApp, and Facebook, generating widespread public attention. According to Stronda, the image was accessed after his wife accidentally left her old phone at a shopping mall, where it was subsequently stolen and hacked.[7] The leak drew commentary not only because of the violation of privacy but also due to the notable size of his genitalia. Stronda publicly addressed the incident, explaining that the photo was leaked after his wife lost her old phone, which contained the image. He expressed regret over the situation but also made light of it, joking about the "three legs" meme that emerged in response to the photo.[8] The incident sparked a wave of satirical content, including image macros, react videos, and internet slang referencing the episode. Stronda’s name became associated with viral notoriety and was incorporated into digital culture, especially among Brazilian meme communities.[9]
