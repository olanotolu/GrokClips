It's a Beautiful Day (Michael Bublé song)

"It's a Beautiful Day" is a song by Canadian singer Michael Bublé, released as the first single from his sixth studio album, To Be Loved (2013). The song was written by Bublé, Alan Chang and Amy S. Foster, and produced by Bob Rock. The single was released on iTunes on February 25, 2013, by 143 Records and Reprise Records. It peaked at number ten on the UK Singles Chart and it was certified gold by the Federation of the Italian Music Industry. The song peaked at number four on Japan's Billboard Hot 100 and reached the top five on airplay charts in Finland and Israel. Michael Bublé singles chronology Music video

Critical reception

Matt Collar of AllMusic described "It's a Beautiful Day" as a "sunshine pop-sounding" track and one of Bublé's best originals on the album. Music blog POP! Goes the Charts described the song as "surprisingly cheerful", noting its full arrangement and descriptive lyrics, and concluding that it would be a "huge seller".

Music Video

Music video

A music video to accompany the release of "It's a Beautiful Day" was first released onto YouTube on March 25, 2013, at a total length of three minutes and forty seconds. Directed by Mark Klasfeld, it was filmed on Colonial Street, the same street that appeared as Wisteria Lane in the series Desperate Housewives. Actress Jaime Pressly plays Bublé's unfaithful girlfriend in the video. The video begins with Bublé catching his partner cheating on him in a house. Afterwards, Bublé calmly walks out of the house and starts performing the song while he walks down the street. While performing the song, he encounters many people, most of them being women who appear to be expressing romantic interest on Bublé, while his partner watches him from the window with discomfort. At the end of the video, Bublé is seen in front of an exploding house. The video includes a few computer-generated images such as an animated bird perching on Bublé's finger and clouds with various animal shapes. As of October 2025, the music video has 69 million views on YouTube.[1]

Live performances

Bublé performed the song on the German television show Wetten, dass..? on March 23, 2013.[2] He also performed the song on Ant & Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway on March 30, 2013. During the performance, Ant & Dec, Simon Cowell, and Lewis Hamilton departed the ITV studios in a golf buggy and entered a pub. Bublé discovered cardboard cutouts of Ant & Dec in the studio, then joined his band to walk to the pub and performed a karaoke version of "Let's Get Ready to Rhumble". Bublé performed a swing version of "It's a Beautiful Day" on the Week 5 results show of The X Factor (U.S. season 3) on November 28, 2013. He performed the song on The Graham Norton Show on April 12, 2013,[3] and at the 2013 Juno Awards on April 21, 2013.[4]
