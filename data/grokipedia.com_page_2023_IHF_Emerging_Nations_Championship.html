2023 IHF Emerging Nations Championship

The 2023 IHF Men's Emerging Nations Championship was the fourth edition of the IHF Emerging Nations Championship held in Bulgaria under the aegis of International Handball Federation from 25 to 30 April 2023.[1] Cuba won the tournament for the first time by defeating Cyprus in the final.[2]
