List of countries by energy consumption and production

1 quadrillion BTU/yr = 1.055 EJ/yr = 293 TWh/yr = 33.433 GW The numbers below are for the total energy consumption or production in a whole year, so should be multiplied by 33.433 to get the average value in GW in that year.
