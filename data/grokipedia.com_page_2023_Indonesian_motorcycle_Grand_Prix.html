2023 Indonesian motorcycle Grand Prix

The 2023 Indonesian motorcycle Grand Prix (officially known as the Pertamina Grand Prix of Indonesia) was the fifteenth round of the 2023 Grand Prix motorcycle racing season. It was held at the Pertamina Mandalika International Street Circuit in Mandalika on 15 October 2023. After Jorge Martín's win in the MotoGP sprint, Ducati secured its fourth consecutive Constructors' Championship in the MotoGP class.[1]
