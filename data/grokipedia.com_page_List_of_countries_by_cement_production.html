Recent Production (as of 2023)

Cement production in Asia and the Pacific continued to dominate global output, accounting for approximately 75% of the world total of 4,100 million metric tons in 2023. Key producers included China (2,100 million metric tons), India (410 million metric tons), Vietnam (110 million metric tons), Indonesia (62 million metric tons), Japan (50 million metric tons), and South Korea (50 million metric tons). Detailed country-level data for all nations post-2012 is available in individual USGS country reports and annual Mineral Commodity Summaries.[5]
