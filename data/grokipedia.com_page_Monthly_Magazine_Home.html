Monthly Magazine Home

Monthly Magazine Home (Korean: 월간 집) is a South Korean television series directed by Lee Chang-min and starring Jung So-min, Kim Ji-seok, Jung Gun-joo and Kim Won-hae.[2] It is a romantic comedy about a real estate CEO who buys houses as investments and a magazine editor who dreams of owning her own home, exploring their differing views on what makes a house a home.[3] It was premiered on JTBC on June 16, 2021, and aired every Wednesday and Thursday at 21:00 (KST) till August 5, 2021.[4][5] It is also available for streaming on iQIYI.

Synopsis

Monthly Magazine Home is a romantic comedy drama that explores contrasting views on home and housing through the lives of the staff at the lifestyle magazine Monthly House, which is facing financial difficulties. Na Young-won (Jung So-min), a dedicated senior editor and housing columnist at the magazine, has always lived modestly in rented homes and believes a house is a place for family and emotional connection, shaped by her modest upbringing. Yu Ja-sung (Kim Ji-seok), the CEO of the magazine's publishing company and a shrewd real estate investor, views houses purely as assets for profit and shelter, a perspective stemming from his own impoverished childhood. Their paths cross when Ja-sung acquires the building where Young-won lives, making him her new landlord, leading to an unexpected romance amid efforts to revive the magazine. The series also follows the personal and professional stories of the magazine's quirky editors as they tackle housing-related issues and relationships.[6][7]

Production

Casting

On April 20, 2020, Jung So-min received an offer to play Na Young-won, a magazine editor with 10 years experience for the magazine 'Home'.[22] On September 22, 2020, Chae Jung-an considered to play the 13-year editor of the magazine.[23] In October 2020, the cast line up was announced.[9] In January, 2021, the members of the 'Monthly House' were revealed by the production crew.[24] Jung So-min and Kim Ji-seok have previously starred together in the 2013 KBS drama special You Came to Me and Became a Star, this is their second time working together.[25] Chae Jung-an is appearing in the TV series after a hiatus of 2 years. She last appeared in 2019 JTBC series Legal High.[26]

Filming

Principal photography of the series began in the second half of 2020 and the filming ended on April 1, 2021.[27] The photos of Jung So-min and Kim Ji-seok from filming site were released on February 23, 2021.[28]

Release

On April 27, 2021, the release date of the series along with a teaser poster was announced.[29] The series premiered on June 16, 2021, on JTBC at 21:00 KST.[29] It is also available for streaming on iQIYI.[30] The rating is 9.5/10 on iQIYI as of October 2025.[31]
