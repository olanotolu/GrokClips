Hahm Eun-jung filmography

Hahm Eun-jung (known mononymously as Eunjung) is a South Korean singer and actress. She is a member of girl group T-ara and its subgroup T-ara N4. In 2012, Eunjung became the first idol star to host the Korean remake of the hit TV show SNL. The episode drew a record-breaking nationwide rating of 1.444%, the show's all-time highest rating at the time.[1] In 2015, it was announced that Hahm would star in the Thai romantic comedy film Micro Love, making her the first female K-pop idol to lead a Thai movie (released in 2017).[2] She has continued her acting career into the 2020s, with recent roles in the 2024 drama Tarot and the 2025 series Queen's House.[3][4]
