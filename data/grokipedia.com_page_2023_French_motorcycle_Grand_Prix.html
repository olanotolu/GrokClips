2023 French motorcycle Grand Prix

The 2023 French motorcycle Grand Prix (officially known as the Shark Grand Prix de France) was the fifth round of the 2023 Grand Prix motorcycle racing season and the first round of the 2023 MotoE World Championship. The main races for MotoGP, Moto2, and Moto3 were held on 14 May 2023, while the Sprint races for those classes and both MotoE races took place on 13 May, all at the Bugatti Circuit in Le Mans, France. The weekend marked the 1000th round of Grand Prix motorcycle racing since its inception in 1949.[1][2]
