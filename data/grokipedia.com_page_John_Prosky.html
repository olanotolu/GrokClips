John Prosky

John Prosky (born 1962) is an American film, theatre, and television actor.

Career

Prosky has had an extensive career in theatre, including performances at the Antaeus Theatre Company (e.g., The Crucible, Tonight at 8:30), Arena Stage (Galileo, Our Town), and Guthrie Theater (Candida). He also contributed to education outreach as head of programs at Antaeus, creating workshops for underserved youth.[1] In 2019, he appeared in Unvarnished Theater Company's production of The Realistic Joneses in Savannah.[2] Prosky's numerous TV credits include NYPD Blue, ER, Heroes, Criminal Minds, True Blood, JAG, My So-Called Life, Star Trek: Deep Space Nine, The Practice, The X-Files, The West Wing, Charmed, 24, House, Grey's Anatomy, Veronica Mars, Just Shoot Me!, Fringe, and the web series Red Bird (2016). More recent television appearances include Suits: L.A. (2025), Found (2023), and Perry Mason (2020).[3] His film credits include The Nutty Professor, Bowfinger, and A.I. Artificial Intelligence. Prosky also contributed voice work to the L.A. Noire video game.[4]

Personal life

Prosky is the son of the late actor Robert Prosky.[5] He has been married to actress Kimiko Gelman since September 17, 2000.[3] Prosky and Gelman have one son, Joseph Kai Prosky.[5]
