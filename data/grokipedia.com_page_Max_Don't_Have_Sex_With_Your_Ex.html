Max Don't Have Sex With Your Ex

E-Rotic singles chronology "Max Don't Have Sex With Your Ex " is a song recorded by German Eurodance act E-Rotic, released in June 1994 by Blow Up as the group's debut and lead single from their first album, Sex Affairs (1995). Written and composed by David Brandes, John O'Flynn, and Felix Gauder, the song hit success in Germany where it peaked at number seven. In the Netherlands, it reached number four while in Austria, it reached number 12. It also peaked at number 14 in Switzerland and number 20 in France. The song is the group's highest-charting single in the United Kingdom, where it peaked at number 45 on the UK Singles Chart in June 1995.

Critical reception

Music Week editor Alan Jones commented, "A naff title for sure, but it's a storming house record."[1] James Hamilton from the Record Mirror Dance Update declared it as a "nagging girl warned (quite rightly as 'It will make your life complex') although otherwise idiotically simple jaunty Euro smash".[2]

Music video

The accompanying music video for "Max Don't Have Sex With Your Ex" was animated and directed by Zoran BihaÄ‡ and Hartcore.[3] The camera descends onto Max's wife sleeping alone as Max is seen cheating on her. She later opens her eyes and discovers her husband cheating on her in his ex's apartment. The video later depicts Max and his mistress at the pool of her house, on a planet resembling a breast, and at a subway station. The wife attempts to catch them in the act using a telescope and while she takes the train at night. The video was A-listed on Germany's VIVA in December 1994.[4]
