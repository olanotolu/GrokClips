2023 IMSA VP Racing SportsCar Challenge

Parent series:
IMSA SportsCar Championship
Michelin Pilot Challenge The 2023 IMSA VP Racing SportsCar Challenge was the inaugural season of the IMSA VP Racing SportsCar Challenge. The series featured LMP3 prototypes and GSX (GT4) cars competing in 45-minute sprint races across six double-header event weekends. The season began on January 20 at Daytona International Speedway and concluded on October 14 at Road Atlanta. Bijoy Garg won the LMP3 class Drivers' Championship after an impressive season taking eight wins throughout the 12-race season, 6 of which he won in a row.[1] His team Jr III Racing won the LMP3 Teams' Championship. Francis Selldorff won the GSX class Drivers' Championship with 1 win and 7 additional podiums. [2] simultaneously granting Turner Motorsport one of their professional championship titles. [3]

Calendar

The provisional 2023 calendar was released on August 5, 2022, at IMSA's annual State of the Sport Address, featuring twelve rounds split across six race weekends. Five of the six events had confirmed dates.[4] The final calendar was confirmed on August 26, 2022, adding rounds at Daytona and Lime Rock Park while dropping the round at WeatherTech Raceway Laguna Seca.[5]
