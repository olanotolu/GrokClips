List of cricket grounds in Australia

This is a list of cricket grounds in Australia. The list includes all grounds that have been used for Test, One Day International, Twenty20 International, first-class, List A and domestic Twenty20 cricket matches. Grounds that have hosted international cricket games are listed in bold. The Melbourne Cricket Ground has hosted the most first-class games in Australia.[1] The Melbourne Cricket Ground has hosted the most List A games. The Melbourne Cricket Ground has hosted the most games overall in Australia.[2]
