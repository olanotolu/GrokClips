List of countries by avocado production

This article lists countries by avocado production from 2016 to 2022, using data provided by the Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database (FAOSTAT), with 2022 being the latest year available as of 2024.[1] Global avocado production in 2022 was 8,978,275 metric tonnes, representing a 3.4% increase from 8,685,672 tonnes in 2021.[1] Mexico remained the leading producer, contributing approximately 28% of the global total. Dependent territories are shown in italics.
