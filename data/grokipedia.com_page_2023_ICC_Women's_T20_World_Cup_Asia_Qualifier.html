2023 Women's T20 World Cup Asia Qualifier

The 2023 ICC Women's T20 World Cup Asia Qualifier was a cricket tournament that formed part of the qualification process for the 2024 ICC Women's T20 World Cup. The Asia Qualifier was hosted by Malaysia in August and September 2023. The two teams to reach the final of the qualifier tournament progressed to the global qualifier. Thailand and United Arab Emirates progressed to the global qualifier after winning their respective semi-finals. United Arab Emirates defeated Thailand in the final by 6 runs.
