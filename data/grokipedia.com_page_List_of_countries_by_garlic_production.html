List of countries by garlic production

This is a list of countries by garlic production from 2016 to 2022, based on data from the Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database.[1] The total world production for garlic in 2022 was 29,149,438 metric tonnes, up slightly from 29,028,027 tonnes in 2021.[1] China was by far the largest producer, accounting for 73% of world production at 21,337,798[1] tonnes.
