List of countries by 4G LTE penetration

This is a list of countries by 4G LTE penetration, defined as the proportion of time mobile users spend connected to LTE networks or the percentage of mobile connections using 4G LTE technology, based on measurements from sources like OpenSignal. The article presents historical rankings from 2013 to 2021.[1]
