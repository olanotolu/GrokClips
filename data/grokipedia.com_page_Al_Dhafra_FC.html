Al Dhafra FC

Al Dhafra FC (Arabic: نادي الظفرة لكرة القدم) is an Emirati professional football club based in Madinat Zayed that competes in the UAE Pro League.[2] Their futsal club participated in AFC Futsal Club Championship.

History

Al Dhafra FC was established in 2000, as part of a policy to promote sports across the country and the Al Dhafra region of Abu Dhabi had no team, so Al Dhafra is the first and only club to be located in the western region.[3] From its founding until entering the UAE Pro League in the 2010–11 season, the club was classified as a semi-professional club according to the UAE Pro League committee and did not have licenses to compete in AFC competitions. The club appealed for professional licensing status after qualifying for the UAE President's Cup for the first time in 2019.[4] In July 2020, Al Dhafra was initially denied a national club license due to financial non-compliance but successfully appealed. Around October 2020, 14 clubs, including Al Dhafra, obtained AFC club licenses, enabling eligible UAE Pro League teams to participate in Asian competitions.[5][6] The club competed in the UAE Pro League from 2010–11 to 2022–23, achieving a best finish of 6th place in 2019–20, before relegation after finishing 14th in 2022–23. Al Dhafra then won the 2024–25 UAE First Division League, earning promotion back to the Pro League for the 2025–26 season.[7]
