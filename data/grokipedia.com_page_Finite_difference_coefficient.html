Finite difference coefficient

In mathematics, finite difference coefficients are the numerical weights used in finite difference formulas to approximate the derivatives of a function to arbitrary order of accuracy. These coefficients can be derived for central, forward, or backward finite differences, depending on the stencil configuration.
