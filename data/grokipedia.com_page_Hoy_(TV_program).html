Hoy (TV program)

Hoy is a Mexican morning television show produced by Televisa and broadcast on Las Estrellas. Since its first broadcast, on August 3, 1998, the program has had several stages. It is the longest-running morning program on Mexican television. The sections focus on entertainment, horoscopes, the summary of soap operas, beauty, cooking, health, games, humor and music.[1]
