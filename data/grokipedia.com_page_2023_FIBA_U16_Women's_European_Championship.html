2023 FIBA U16 Women's European Championship

The 2023 FIBA U16 Women's European Championship was the 33rd edition of the FIBA U16 Women's European Championship for women's national under-16 teams. The tournament was played from 11 to 19 August 2023 in İzmir, Turkey.[1] The top five teams qualified for the 2024 FIBA Under-17 Women's Basketball World Cup in Mexico.

Participating teams

  • Belgium
  • Croatia
  • Czech Republic
  • Finland
  • France
  • Greece
  • Hungary
  • Israel (Third place, 2022 FIBA U16 Women's European Championship Division B)
  • Italy
  • Latvia
  • Poland
  • Portugal
  • Serbia (Winners, 2022 FIBA U16 Women's European Championship Division B)
  • Slovenia
  • Spain
  • Turkey (Runners-up, 2022 FIBA U16 Women's European Championship Division B)

2023 FIBA Under-16 Women's European Championship Division A – First Round

The draw of the first round was held on 14 February 2023 in Freising, Germany.[2][3] In the first round, the teams were drawn into four groups of four. All teams advanced to the playoffs. All times are local (Turkey Time – UTC+3).
