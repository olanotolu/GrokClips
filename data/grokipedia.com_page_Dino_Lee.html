Dino Lee

Dino Lee (born Lee Yu-hsi (Chinese: 李玉璽; Pe̍h-ōe-jī: Lí Gio̍k-sú) on 31 March 1993), also known as Lee Yuk-Sai, is a Taiwanese singer, composer, musician and actor. He is the son of Taiwanese singer Li Ya Ming.[1] He is known for his role as Ouyang Feifan in the 2015 film Our Times.[2]
