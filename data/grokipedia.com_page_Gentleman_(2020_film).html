Gentleman (2020 film)

Gentleman is a 2020 Indian Kannada-language action thriller film co-written and directed by Jadesh Kumar Hampi in his directorial debut,[1] and produced by Guru Deshpande under his banner Guru Deshpande Productions (G Cinemas).[2][3][4] It features Prajwal Devaraj[5][6][7] and Nishvika Naidu[8][9] along with Sanchari Vijay in the lead roles. Gentleman was released on 7 February 2020 and received positive reviews from critics.

Plot Summary

Plot

Bharath Kumar, who works in an electronics showroom, suffers from sleeping beauty syndrome, which is said to occur in one among a million people. It makes one fall asleep for 18 hours a day, leaving just 6 hours for the rest of their activities, including work. Bharath's family consists of his brother, sister-in-law, and their daughter Varu. One night, Bharath's brother and sister-in-law die in an accident. Due to Bharath's condition, he cannot care for Varu, which leads him to send her to an orphanage. However, she is brought back by Tapaswini, who is Bharath's ex-girlfriend and promises to take care of her. They spend time together and also travel to Wonderla, but Varu goes missing while she is playing there. Worried, Bharath and Tapaswini lodge a complaint with the police. ACP Shivamurthy, along with Constable Nagappa, takes charge of the investigation along with Bharath. They find clues leading to a person named Nani; however, he is declared dead, which leads the case to a dead end. Bharath investigates the case himself. At night, Bharath hears a man named Johnny reciting a song Chikki Chikki Chikkappa, Chochlategintha sweetappa.. Chikki Chikki Chikkappa, nidde enda ellappa which was sung by Varu to Bharath. Bharath chases Johnny, who escapes due to Bharath's syndrome. Bharath, along with Shivamurthy, arrives at Johnny's house. After a thorough search, he finds a medicine sticker which was found at his brother's shirt. After meeting with Tapaswini's senior, he learns that the medicine is used for ova scam purposes. After interrogating the medicine supplier, Bharath finds that Dr. Swaminathan Rai is the main mastermind behind the scam. With the help of Shivamurthy's contacts with the Police Control station and Nagappa, he tracks the ambulance, which leads to a hideout where the girls are kept. There, Bharath confronts Swaminathan, who reveals that he killed his brother and sister-in-law after a girl in their hideout escaped and had requested them to take her to a police station. Enraged, Bharath fights and defeats Swaminathan and his henchmen, including Johnny, rescues Varu, and exposes the ova trafficking ring. Bharath and Varu emotionally reconcile, and he reunites with Tapaswini.

Production

Principal photography for the film was scheduled to begin on 25 October 2018.[13] The production involved six months of research into sleeping beauty syndrome and illegal ova scams.[1][2] Filming took place at real locations, including garbage dumps and drainage tunnels in Mysuru, on a limited budget; the team even used an earth mover to create a road at one site.[2] The muhurath (launch ceremony) was held on 4 July 2018, with actor Darshan as the chief guest, and the first schedule was planned for the first week of August.[14]

Release

The film's first look poster was released on 4 July 2018, coinciding with lead actor Prajwal Devaraj's birthday.[14] The first song from the film was released on 27 December 2019.[15] The second song from the film was released on 16 January 2020.[16] The trailer was released by Puneeth Rajkumar and Dhruva Sarja on 6 January 2020.[17][18] The film's wake-up theme song "Yeddelo Bharathiya", sung by Anthony Daasan, was released on 29 January 2020.[19] Before release, the film generated interest for remakes in the Tamil and Telugu industries.[20][21][22][23] No remakes were ultimately produced as of 2025. The film was theatrically released on 7 February 2020.[24]
