John Rooney (footballer)

Club domestic league appearances and goals as of 27 April 2025, 07:23 (UTC) John Richard Rooney (born 17 December 1990) is an English professional manager and former footballer who played as an attacking midfielder. He is head coach of National League North club, Macclesfield. He is the younger brother of former England forward Wayne Rooney. Although born in England, Rooney expressed a desire to represent the Republic of Ireland at international level.

On 20 July 2020, Rooney signed for National League side Stockport County for an undisclosed fee.

Return to Barrow

On 30 January 2022, Rooney returned to League Two side Barrow for an undisclosed fee on a two-and-a-half-year deal.
