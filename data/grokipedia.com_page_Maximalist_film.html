Maximalist film

Maximalist film or maximalist cinema is a filmmaking aesthetic characterized by excess and abundance, serving as a reaction against minimalism. It adopts a "more is more" philosophy, emphasizing spectacle, scale, bombast, elaborate visuals, and sensory overload. Collider Wikipedia

Maximalism

In the arts, maximalism, a reaction against minimalism, is an aesthetic of excess.[1] The philosophy can be summarized as "more is more", contrasting with the minimalist motto "less is more".
