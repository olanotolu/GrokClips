The Italy national U-17 football team is the national under-17 football team of Italy and is controlled by the Italian Football Federation. The team competes in the UEFA European Under-17 Championship, held every year. Italy qualified for the 2025 finals in Albania, finishing third. If qualified, the team also competes in the FIFA U-17 World Cup, which is held biennially; Italy has qualified for the 2025 edition in Qatar.[3]
