List of current NRL Women's team squads

Below is a list of current NRL Women's team squads for the 2025 season. There are 12 teams that compete in the NRL Women's Premiership, which expanded to 10 teams in 2023 from 6 in 2022 and to 12 teams in 2025 with the addition of the Canterbury-Bankstown Bulldogs and North Queensland Cowboys.[1]

Brisbane Broncos

The Brisbane Broncos are coached by Scott Prince.[2] The Broncos won the 2025 NRL Women's Premiership, defeating the Sydney Roosters 22-18 in the Grand Final on October 5, 2025.[3] Jersey numbers in the table reflect the team list announced for Round 8.
