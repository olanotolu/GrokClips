Cytherea (actress)

Cytherea is an American pornographic actress and model. She is known for her ability to ejaculate (known as "squirting" in the adult industry) while performing sex acts.

Career

Cytherea took her stage name from the Greek goddess of love, beauty, and music.[1][2] She originally had a Yahoo! fan group when she was recruited by an agent into the adult industry. Her video career started in 2003,[3] and she has performed in over 350 movies. Her first scene was with Tyce Bune for I've Never Done That Before 14. Elegant Angel produced a series of movies featuring her dubbed as "Squirtwoman".[2] Cytherea performed her first anal scene in the movie Cytherea's Anal Whores in 2006.[3] In 2005, she received the AVN Best New Starlet Award. Appearances

She has appeared on cable television shows, most notably the HBO/Cinemax series Sex Games: Vegas , Playboy TV's Night Calls and has been a guest on the Tom Leykis Show[2] and The Howard Stern Show.[1] Cytherea also hosted an industry talk and interview radio show on KSEX in Los Angeles, California called Goddess of Gush.[10] According to her website, the show was cancelled in 2006 and Cytherea has toured as a feature dancer in the past. She has also had minor roles in a variety of movies such as the thriller Tumbling After[11] and the comedy film Finishing the Game: The Search for a New Bruce Lee ,[12] both in 2007, and the cable television series Sex Games Vegas in 2005.[13]

Personal life

Cytherea was born in Salt Lake City, Utah.[1] She is a former Mormon.[14] She self-identifies as bisexual.[14] Cytherea's house was invaded and robbed on January 19, 2015, by four armed teenagers, three of whom sexually assaulted her four times. Police believed the house was selected at random.[15][16] Cytherea, her husband, and her two children were all home at the time.[17][16][18] She was married to Timothy Hale. Hale died at the age of 52 on November 2, 2016.[19]
