List of current NCAA Division I baseball coaches

The following is a list of current NCAA Division I baseball coaches. As of 2025, 303 programs compete at the Division I level in NCAA college baseball.[1] Each program employs a head coach. The longest-tenured head coach is Tim Sinicki, who has been the head coach at Binghamton since the start of the 1993 season.[2]
