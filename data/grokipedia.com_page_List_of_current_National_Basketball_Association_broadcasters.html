National broadcasters

English announcers

National television rights in English language in the United States are held by ESPN/ABC, NBC Sports and Prime Video, with ESPN Deportes broadcasting games in the Spanish language. During the regular season, ESPN and NBC air most nationally televised games. Most national games on ESPN are available on television, with select games streamed online exclusively on ESPN+. ABC will also air a select number of games nationally.[4]
