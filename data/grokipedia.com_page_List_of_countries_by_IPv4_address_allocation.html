List of countries by IPv4 address allocation

This is a list of countries by IPv4 address allocation. The Internet Assigned Numbers Authority (IANA) distributes large blocks of addresses to regional Internet registries (RIRs), which then assign them to national Internet registries and local Internet registries within their respective service regions.[1] Allocations are attributed to countries according to the registered location of the recipient organizations or registries. While IPv4 allows for a theoretical maximum of 232 addresses (approximately 4.3 billion), the number available for public use is smaller due to reserved blocks for private and special purposes.[2] As of 2025, the entire IPv4 address space has been allocated or reserved, with no unallocated blocks remaining.[2]
