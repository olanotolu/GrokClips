2023 FIBA Basketball World Cup broadcasting rights

The 2023 FIBA Basketball World Cup was a basketball tournament that took place from 25 August to 10 September 2023 involving 32 men's national teams from FIBA-affiliated nations. The tournament was broadcast worldwide. According to FIBA, broadcasters from 190 countries and territories secured rights to the tournament.[1][2]
