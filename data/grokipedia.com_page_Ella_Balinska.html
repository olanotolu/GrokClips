Ella Balinska

Ella Balinska (born 4 October 1996) is an English actress and DJ. She is known for her roles in the action-comedy film Charlie's Angels (2019), the Netflix series Resident Evil (2022), voicing the protagonist Frey Holland in the video game Forspoken (2023), and starring in the films Skincare (2024) and The Occupant (2025) [1] [2].

Early life and education

Balinska was born on 4 October 1996 in London to Polish musician and entrepreneur Kaz Balinski Jundzill and Jamaican-British chef Lorraine Pascale.[3][4][5] She has described her background as being a mix of "British, Polish and Caribbean".[6] Her parents divorced in 2000.[4] Her father is married to model Sophie Anderton.[7] Balinska played county-level netball[8] and competed in track and field at a national level,[9] having been the javelin thrower for Team London.[10] She went to James Allen's Girls' School in Dulwich and left in 2015 to attend Arts Educational School (ArtsEd) in Chiswick for Sixth Form.[11] She then went on to train at the Guildford School of Acting.[8] She left during her final semester in 2018 for work and returned in 2020 to complete her degree, officially graduating with a Bachelor of Arts in Acting in 2021.[12] The Ella Balinska Scholarship was established in her name.[13]

Career

In 2017, Balinska obtained the lead role in the Sky Kids series Nyela's Dream (2019) as Nyela Malik.[14] She starred alongside Kristen Stewart and Naomi Scott in the action comedy Charlie's Angels, a continuation of the 1976 television series of the same name and two previous theatrical films.[15] It was released in November 2019, garnering mixed critical reviews.[16][17] Balinska starred in the thriller film Run Sweetheart Run, which premiered at the 2020 Sundance Film Festival.[18] She also starred in Resident Evil, a Netflix live-action television series based on the video game series of the same name. It was released in July 2022, which was canceled after one season.[19] Balinska portrayed and voiced Frey Holland, the protagonist of the action role-playing video game Forspoken (2023).[20] She starred as Abby in the science fiction thriller film The Occupant (2025).[21] In 2024, Balinska appeared as Jessica in the comedy film Skincare.[1] In 2025, she starred in the thriller Marked Men: Rule + Shaw.[22] She is set to lead in the horror film The Horror Game.[23] In 2022, Balinska began DJing and producing techno music.[24][25]

Personal life

Balinska is the founding patron of the BFC Foundation, the charitable arm of the British Fashion Council.[26] In 2020, Balinska established the Ella Balinska Scholarship at the Guildford School of Acting to support Black, Asian, and minority ethnic drama students.[12][13]
