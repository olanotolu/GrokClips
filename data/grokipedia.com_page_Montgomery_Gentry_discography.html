Montgomery Gentry discography

Montgomery Gentry is an American country music duo founded by Eddie Montgomery and Troy Gentry. Following Troy Gentry's death in 2017, Eddie Montgomery has continued releasing music under the Montgomery Gentry name. Its discography comprises 11 studio albums, five compilation albums, three extended plays, and 41 singles. The duo made its debut in 1999 with the single "Hillbilly Shoes," which went to number 13 on the Hot Country Songs charts, but did not reach number one until mid-2004, with "If You Ever Stop Loving Me." The duo has sent four more singles to Number One for a total of five: "Something to Be Proud Of" (2005), "Lucky Man" (2007), "Back When I Knew It All", and "Roll with Me" (both 2008). Besides these, Montgomery Gentry has reached the Top 10 with 10 additional hit singles. All 15 of these songs have charted on the Billboard Hot 100, where the duo's highest peak is "If You Ever Stop Loving Me" at number 30. Recent releases include the 2024 EP Home Run and singles such as "Cost of Being Me" (2023).
