2023 Hougang United FC season

The 2023 season was Hougang United's 26th consecutive season in the Singapore Premier League. After finishing third in the 2022 Singapore Premier League and winning the 2022 Singapore Cup, Hougang qualified for the 2023–24 AFC Cup. Hougang finished sixth in the Singapore Premier League, were runners-up in the Singapore Cup, and finished last in their 2023–24 AFC Cup group. The women's team competed in the 2023 Women's Premier League, finishing third.[1][2]

Hougang United FC 2023 Season

After winning the 2022 Singapore Cup, Hougang was thought to be a contender for the SPL champion.[3] On 6 April 2023, Hougang lost to Tanjong Pagar United FC 1-2 and was second bottom of the SPL table with the same points as Young Lions but with a better goal difference.[3] On 17 April, assistant coach Marko Kraljević replaced head coach Firdaus Kassim as interim head coach after the club failed to win any matches since they won the opening match in the 2023 Singapore Premier League season.[4] Firdaus was redeployed to manage a youth team.[4] Despite the coaching change, Hougang struggled throughout the season, finishing 6th in the SPL with 29 points from 24 matches. They reached the Singapore Cup final but lost 1-3 to Lion City Sailors on 9 December 2023. In the 2023–24 AFC Cup group stage, Hougang finished last in Group H and were eliminated.
