New Model Army discography

New Model Army discography The discography of New Model Army, a British rock band which formed in 1980, consists of sixteen studio albums, eight live albums, ten compilation albums, five extended plays and twenty singles, which were released by Abstract Records, EMI Records, Epic Records, Attack Attack Records, and earMUSIC, as well as seven video albums (as of October 2025).[1]
