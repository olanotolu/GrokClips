Kids' Choice Award for Favorite Male TV Star

The Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards, held annually on United States television since 1988 except in 1993, includes a category for Favorite Male TV Star. Originally a single category, it was split into Favorite Male TV Star (Kids) and Favorite Male TV Star (Family) in 2016, reverted to one category from 2017 to 2021, and split again from 2022 onward.
