Max Collins (actress)

In this Philippine name, the middle name or maternal family name is Abiera and the surname or paternal family name is Collins. Isabelle Abiera Collins (born August 28, 1992), known professionally as Max Collins, is an American-born Filipino actress and model. She is known for her roles in GMA Network series and her appearance as Ann Villegas in the international series Almost Paradise (2023). She is currently an exclusive artist of GMA Network.

Early life

Collins was born in California.[1] Her mother is a Filipino who is from Kalibo, Aklan. Her father is an American of Irish-Italian descent. Her parents met in Los Angeles when her mother was working at Bloomingdale's. Her parents separated when she was four years old. Her mother remarried when she was 9. She has a younger half-brother.[2] Collins used to live in Vallejo, California. When she was 10, her family moved to Kalibo, Aklan. For four years she lived in Boracay and attended Brent School Boracay.[2]

Career

2006â€“2010: Early years in ABSâ€“CBN

Collins started doing commercials when she was 10. She has also appeared in magazines like Meg, Candy, Mega, People and Seventeen. According to her, she was discovered by a talent scout when she accidentally walked into a shop while a shoot was ongoing. After a year she was brought to ABS-CBN, tried out a VTR with Star Magic, and got a call back after a month. Eventually she qualified for Star Magic Batch 15.[3] Collins was just 13 when she was launched as a Star Magic Batch 15 member. Under her stage name Isabelle Abiera, she did small roles in ABS-CBN's Star Magic Presents: Astigs and Star Magic Presents:Abt Ur Luv Ur Lyf 2. She also had a short hosting stint in Channel V. However, her entertainment career was put on a hold when she left for the United States.[4] Collins made a comeback into the Philippine entertainment industry as one of the regular hosts of the defunct noontime program Wowowee, at the same time she appeared as Veronica in the afternoon TV series Rosalka.[4] She played a planted gate crasher in Pinoy Big Brother: Teen Clash 2010. When Rosalka ended, she went on to play Christy in Precious Hearts Romances Presents: Alyna.

2011â€“present: Transfer to GMA Network

In 2010, when her contract with Star Magic ended, Collins left for the United States to figure things out. A year after, she returned to the Philippines to relaunch her entertainment career. She changed her agent to Perry Lansigan's PPL Entertainment, Inc., changed her stage name to Max Collins, and finally signed an exclusive contract with GMA Network.[5] In 2012, she starred in a supporting role in her first afternoon series under GMA Network, The Good Daughter, playing Bea's (Kylie Padilla) best friend and secret lover of Darwin. She also appeared in some episodes of Maynila. That same year, she had a role in the primetime series Pahiram ng Sandali alongside Dingdong Dantes, Lorna Tolentino, and Boyet de Leon. In 2014, Collins landed her first lead role in the afternoon series Innamorata. She continued with supporting and lead parts in GMA shows, including Someone to Watch Over Me (2015) and a role in the fantasy reboot Encantadia (2016).[6] Her film career gained traction with roles in Bamboo Flowers (2013),[7] Remembering Ada (2016),[8][9] and Citizen Jake (2018), an entry in the Metro Manila Film Festival.[10] Collins expanded internationally with a role in the Hollywood series Almost Paradise (2020).[11] She received acclaim for lead roles in GMA primetime series such as The One That Got Away (2018), Bihag (2019), and To Have and to Hold (2021). In 2024, she joined the cast of the historical drama Pulang Araw.[12] As of 2025, she stars in upcoming projects including Akusada and Only We Know.

Personal life

Collins is a cousin of actresses Lauren Young and Megan Young.[13] She was close friends with actress Jessy Mendiola.[2] Collins was baptized as a Born-again Christian in October 2015.[14] Collins married Pancho Magno in Manila on December 11, 2017.[15] They have a son, Skye Anakin, who was born on July 6, 2020.[16][17] In May 2023, Collins confirmed her split with Magno.[18] They divorced in October 2024.[19]
