Al-Ramtha SC

Al-Ramtha Sports Club (Arabic: نادي الرمثا الرياضي) is a professional football club based in Ar Ramtha, Jordan. They compete in the Jordanian Pro League, the top flight of Jordanian football.

Al-Ramtha SC Stadium

Stadium

Al-Ramtha SC plays its home matches in the Jordanian Pro League at Al-Hassan Stadium in Irbid, which has a capacity of 12,000 spectators.[4] The club's official stadium is Prince Hashim Stadium in Ar Ramtha, with a capacity of 5,000.[2] Prince Hashim Stadium is also the home stadium of Ittihad Al-Ramtha. As of October 2025, Al-Hassan Stadium is closed for 3-month maintenance, affecting home fixtures.[5]
