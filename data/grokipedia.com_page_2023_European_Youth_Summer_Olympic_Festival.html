2023 European Youth Summer Olympic Festival

The 2023 European Youth Summer Olympic Festival was the seventeenth edition of this biennial multi-sport competition organized by the European Olympic Committees for athletes aged 14 to 18, held from 23 to 29 July in Maribor, Slovenia.[1][2] The event featured 11 disciplines, including artistic gymnastics, athletics, basketball, cycling, handball, judo, swimming, tennis, and volleyball, among others, with 123 separate events contested by 2,419 athletes representing 48 nations.[2][3] Italy dominated the medal standings, securing the highest number of medals and underscoring the nation's strength in youth development across multiple sports.[4] The festival emphasized not only athletic competition but also educational and cultural programs aimed at fostering Olympic values among participants.[3] No significant controversies marred the proceedings, allowing focus on emerging talents who demonstrated high-level performances in disciplines like swimming and athletics.[5]

Background and Selection

The 2023 European Youth Summer Olympic Festival was the 17th edition of this quadrennial multi-sport event organized by the European Olympic Committees for athletes aged 14 to 18.
