Diomansy Kamara

Diomansy Mehdi Moustapha Kamara (born 8 November 1980) is a former professional footballer who played as a striker. Born in Paris, France, he played internationally for Senegal, whom he represented at three Africa Cup of Nations. During a 17-year professional career, Kamara played for football clubs in Italy, England, Scotland, Turkey and India as well as France.
