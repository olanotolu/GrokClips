Elizabeth Maxwell

Elizabeth Maxwell (born October 13, 1983) is an American voice actress. She is best known for her roles as Ymir in Attack on Titan, Winter Schnee in RWBY, Nikki in Camp Camp, Motoko Kusanagi in Ghost in the Shell Arise, Cordelia Gallo in Gosick, Urbosa & Riju in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild and The Legend of Zelda: Tears Of The Kingdom, Rosaria in Genshin Impact, Caulifla in Dragon Ball Super, Hollyberry Cookie, Light Cream Cookie, and Seaweed Cookie in Cookie Run: Kingdom and Sae Nijima in Persona 5.

Career

After growing up in Northern California, Maxwell moved to Los Angeles to study theater at Chapman University.[3][4] Maxwell began her acting career in short web films, the first being Living Like Fire (2004). She later appeared as Abby in the episode "Amplification" of the television show Criminal Minds (2009). In her late twenties, she relocated from Los Angeles to Austin, Texas, where she began pursuing more voiceover opportunities. She joined Funimation and achieved a breakout role as Ymir in Attack on Titan (2013), followed by the same character in the parody show Attack on Titan: Junior High (2015).[5] Since then, Maxwell has continued to build her career, voicing prominent roles in anime series like My Hero Academia and Overlord, video games including Persona 5 and The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, and web series such as RWBY, while working with studios including Crunchyroll and Rooster Teeth as of 2025.[6]
