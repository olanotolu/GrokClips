Carcharhinus

Temporal range: Lutetian–recent Carcharhinus is the type genus of the family Carcharhinidae, the requiem sharks. One of 12 genera in its family, it contains over half of the species therein. It contains 36 extant and at least nine extinct species as of 2023, including recent additions such as the possibly extinct C. obsoletus (2019) and the extinct C. dicelmai (2022), with likely more species yet to be described.
