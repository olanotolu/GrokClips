Max Eigenmann

In this Philippine name, the middle name or maternal family name is Pimentel and the surname or paternal family name is Eigenmann. Maxene Eve Pimentel Eigenmann (born September 29, 1987), known professionally as Max Eigenmann, is a Filipino actress and singer. She is known for her work in independent films and has received numerous accolades, including the Best Actress award at the 13th Asia Pacific Screen Awards in 2019 for Verdict, Best Actress at the Cinemalaya International Film Festival in 2022 for 12 Weeks, and Best Actress at the ASEAN International Film Festival and Awards in 2023 for Kargo[1][2][3][4]. In 2024, she was nominated for a Gawad Urian Award for her role in Raging Grace[5], and in 2025, she co-hosted the 73rd FAMAS Awards[6].

Personal life

Eigenmann is the daughter of the late actor Mark Gil and former beauty queen-turned-actress Bing Pimentel.[2] Her brother is Sid Lucero. Through her father, she is a half-sister to Andi Eigenmann, Gabby Eigenmann and Ira Eigenmann. Her cousins are Ryan, Geoff and AJ Eigenmann. She has two sons: Sandro Assad (with Jacob Assad) and Massimo Crisologo (with her ex-partner, businessman Norman Crisologo).[7][8][9]
