List of countries by grape production

This is a list of countries by grape production from the years 2017 to 2023, based on data from the Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database.[1] The estimated total world production for grapes in 2023 was 72,500,000 metric tonnes, down from 74,942,573 tonnes in 2022.[2] China was the largest producer of grapes, accounting for 18.6% of global production. Italy came second at 9.2%, followed by France at 8.6%.[1]
