2023 FIBA U20 European Championship

The 2023 FIBA U20 European Championship was the 24th edition of the European basketball championship for men's national under-20 teams. It was played from 8 to 16 July 2023 in Heraklion, Greece.[1]

Participating teams

  • Belgium
  • Croatia
  • Estonia (Third place, 2022 FIBA U20 European Championship Division B)
  • France
  • Germany
  • Greece
  • Iceland (Runner-up, 2022 FIBA U20 European Championship Division B)
  • Israel
  • Italy
  • Lithuania
  • Montenegro
  • Poland
  • Serbia (Winner, 2022 FIBA U20 European Championship Division B)
  • Slovenia
  • Spain
  • Turkey

The draw of the first round was held on 14 February 2023 in Freising, Germany.[2][3] In the first round, the teams were drawn into four groups of four. All teams advance to the playoffs. All times are local (Eastern European Summer Time â€“ UTC+3).
