Aparna Dixit

Aparna Dixit (born 20 October 1991) is an Indian television actress who predominantly appears in Hindi-language television serials. She is best known for playing Purvi in Sony Pal's Yeh Dil Sun Raha Hai , Devika in Life OK's Kalash – Ek Vishwaas , Bani in Colors TV's Bepanah Pyaar [2], Srishti in Dangal's Pyaar Ki Luka Chuppi [3], and Indu in Star Bharat's Woh Toh Hai Albelaa [4].

Early life

Dixit was born on 20 October 1991[5] in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India.[6] She completed her schooling at Holy Public School in Agra and earned a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) Honours in Physics from the University of Delhi.[6] She has a younger brother, Agam Dixit, who is also an actor.[7]

Career

Dixit made her 2013 acting debut in Star Plus's mythological series Mahabharat as Maharani Ambika.[8] She followed this with the role of Manju Naik in Zee TV's Pavitra Rishta from 2013 to 2014.[9][10] In 2014, Dixit portrayed Gauri in Colors TV's Meri Aashiqui Tum Se Hi. Her breakthrough came with a lead role in Sony Pal's Yeh Dil Sun Raha Hai, opposite Navi Bhangu as Purvi,[11][12] this show went off the air in February 2015.[13] She then played the protagonist Devika in Life OK's Kalash – Ek Vishwaas opposite Krip Suri,[14][15] which aired from 2015 until 2017.[16][17] In 2016, she participated in Colors TV's Box Cricket League 2. In 2018, she played Roxana in Sony Entertainment Television's Porus. In October 2018, she reunited with her Kalash co-actor, Krip Suri, for &TV's Laal Ishq, performing an episodic role as Savitri.[18] In 2019, she played the role of Bani in Bepanah Pyaar, alongside Pearl V Puri and Ishita Dutta.[19][2][20] The show ended in February 2020.[21] She later participated in Colors TV's reality show Khatra Khatra Khatra in 2020.[22] From 2019 to 2020, she played the lead of Srishti in Dangal's Pyar Ki Luka Chuppi, opposite Rahul Sharma.[3] The show ended in September 2020.[23] In 2021, she performed in a Hindi language web series titled Exit. From May to October 2022, she portrayed the main antagonist, Anjali Malhotra, in Star Bharat's Woh Toh Hai Albelaa alongside Shaheer Sheikh and Hiba Nawab.[4] Since December 2023, she has played the lead role of Devi Manda (as Dhani Sharma) in Shemaroo TV's Karmadhikari Shanidev, ongoing as of October 2025.[24]

Personal life

Dixit has a younger brother, Agam Dixit, who is also an actor.[7] She has supported his career, including helping with his debut in the television series Sirf Tum. The siblings maintain a close relationship, including family vacations such as a trip to Dubai in September 2024, and social media posts as of October 2025. Dixit remains unmarried. In early July 2022, Dixit contracted chickenpox and stopped shooting for her ongoing series Woh Toh Hai Albelaa until her complete recovery.[4]
