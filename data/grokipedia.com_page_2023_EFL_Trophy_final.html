2023 EFL Trophy final

|---------------------|--------------------------| | Date | 2 April 2023 | | Venue | Wembley Stadium, London | | Referee | Ben Toner | | Attendance | 79,389 | The 2023 EFL Trophy final was the final match of the 2022–23 EFL Trophy, known as the Papa Johns Trophy for sponsorship reasons and the 40th season in the history of the competition.[1] The EFL Trophy is a knock-out tournament for clubs in EFL League One and League Two, the third and fourth tiers of the English football league system, as well as academy teams of 16 Premier League clubs with Category One status. With a crowd of 79,389, the final was the highest attended match in Europe that weekend.[2] Bolton Wanderers defeated Plymouth Argyle 4–0, which was the biggest ever win in an EFL Trophy final.[3][4][5]
