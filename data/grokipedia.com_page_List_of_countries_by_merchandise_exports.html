List of countries by merchandise exports

The following article lists countries and territories by their merchandise exports according to 2024 data from the World Bank and other sources. Merchandise exports are goods whose economic ownership changes from residents to non-residents, including domestically produced goods and re-exports of imported goods. Only physical objects are counted. For example, cars, clothing, machinery, and agricultural products are merchandise exports. Exports of services are excluded.[1][2]
