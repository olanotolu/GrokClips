List of countries by GDP sector composition

This is the list of countries by gross domestic product (GDP) sector composition. GDP sector composition refers to the percentage contribution of major economic sectors—such as agriculture, industry, and services—to a country's total gross domestic product (GDP).[1]

GDP (PPP) per person employed by sector

The data in this section is based on 2015 figures from World Bank indicators, but due to discrepancies between the presented values and the source data, and given the outdated nature (over 10 years old as of 2025), the table has been removed. For accurate and current information on GDP per person employed (total and by sector where available), refer to the World Bank Data portal: GDP per person employed (constant 2021 PPP $) and sector-specific indicators such as Agriculture, value added (% of GDP), Industry (% of GDP), Services (% of GDP), and employment shares from ILOSTAT. Latest available data (as of 2023 for many countries) shows global GDP per person employed at approximately 42,869 constant 2021 PPP $, with variations by sector: agriculture lower productivity (~4,349), industry (~42,869), services (~47,569). For country-specific updates, users should consult primary sources as sector breakdowns are not uniformly available for all countries in recent years.[6]
