Maxime Cressy

Maxime Cressy (born May 8, 1997) is a French-American professional tennis player. He has a career-high singles ranking of world No. 31 by the ATP, achieved on 8 August 2022. He has been ranked as high as world No. 64 in doubles, achieved on 8 May 2023. Cressy has won one title on the ATP Tour and six singles titles and thirteen doubles titles on the ATP Challenger Tour and ITF Futures circuit. Before 2018, he played for his country of birth, France. Cressy is currently inactive from professional tennis due to a back injury, having announced a hiatus from the ATP Tour in July 2025.[1]

Playing style

Cressy is a big server who plays a predominantly serve-and-volley style in his service games.[66] His second serve is nearly as fast as his first serve.[67] He has an aggressive return of serve. He has a good forehand and backhand but is most dangerous when he is chipping-and-charging and volleying.[68]
