Maxime Gonalons

Maxime Gonalons (born 10 March 1989) is a French former professional footballer who played as a defensive midfielder. He spent the majority of his career with Lyon, making 342 appearances and serving as captain, before moving to Roma in 2017. Gonalons had loan spells at Sevilla and Granada, joined the latter permanently in 2020, and ended his playing career at Clermont in 2024, retiring on 16 October 2024.[1]
