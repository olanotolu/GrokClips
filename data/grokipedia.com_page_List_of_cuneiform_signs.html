List of cuneiform signs

Cuneiform is one of the earliest systems of writing, emerging in Sumer in the late fourth millennium BC. Early versions of cuneiform writing from the Early Dynastic period (ED III), such as in literature like the Barton Cylinder[1], and earlier, are not included due to extreme complexity of arranging them consistently and unequivocally by the shape of their signs; see Early Dynastic Cuneiform for the Unicode block. The relevant shape for the classification of a sign is the Neo-Assyrian one (after ca. 1000 BC); the standardization of sign shapes of this late period allows systematic arrangement by shape.
