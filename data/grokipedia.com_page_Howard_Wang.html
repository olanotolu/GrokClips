Howard Wang

Howard Wang (born April 28, 1993)[2] is an American voice actor, known for his work in anime dubs. Wang worked as a voice-over designer at Riot Games from 2019 to 2021 after moving out to California, while building his voice acting career.[1] Outside of voice acting, he also runs a VTuber channel known as Dust XinXue.[3]
