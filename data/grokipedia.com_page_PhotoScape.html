PhotoScape

PhotoScape is a graphics editing program, developed by MOOII Tech, South Korea. The basic concept of PhotoScape is 'easy and fun', allowing users to easily edit photographs taken from their digital cameras or even mobile phones. PhotoScape provides a simple user interface to perform common photo enhancements including color adjustment, cutting, resizing, printing and GIF animation.[2][3] It operates on Microsoft Windows 10 or later and macOS 10.12 or later. The legacy version 3.7 is available on Linux systems as a Snap package.[4] The default languages are English and Korean, with additional language packages available for download. PhotoScape X 4.3.4, released December 20, 2023, is the latest version as of October 2025, supporting Windows 10 and macOS 10.12 or later, with a Pro version available for a fee offering additional features. The legacy version 3.7 remains available for older Windows systems such as XP, Vista, 7, or 8, and even Windows 98 or ME. It is distributed free of charge for all users, including commercial bodies.[5]

Features

PhotoScape 3.7

PhotoScape 3.7 can perform the following tasks:[6]
  • Photo Editor: Enhance and balance color, resize, add effects, overlays and clip-arts.
  • Photo Batch-Editor: Process multiple photos at once, rename multiple photos at once.
  • Collage Creator: Join multiple photos into a poster-like single page or one final photo.
  • GIF Animation: Make multiple images into GIF-animated image.
  • Featured Printer: Print photos for particular occasions, such as passport photos, or lined pages such as graph, calendar or music paper.
  • Screen Capture: Save monitor screen into an image file.
  • Color Picker: Pick color from screen pixel.
  • RAW Converter: Convert RAW format picture into JPEG format.
  • Face Finder: Find similar faces through internet.

PhotoScape X

PhotoScape X, the current version as of October 2025, provides the following key features:
  • Viewer: Browse photos, videos, audio; loupe view, full screen, metadata viewer.
  • Editor: Over 1,000 filters and effects, frames, brushes, rotate, crop, resize, mask.
  • Cut Out: Remove backgrounds using magic eraser, lasso, brush.
  • Batch: Edit, resize, rename multiple files.
  • Collage: Merge photos into one.
  • Combine: Attach photos vertically or horizontally.
  • Animated GIF: Create GIFs from images.
  • Print: Print photos.
  • Screen Capture: Capture full screen, window, or selected area (Windows 10+).
  • Color Picker: Zoom and pick colors.
  • RAW Converter: Convert RAW to JPEG and other formats.
