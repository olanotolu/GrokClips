List of countries by cancer rate

This is a list of countries by cancer rate[1], as measured by incidence rates (new cancer cases per 100,000 population), mortality rates (deaths per 100,000 population), or absolute frequency (total new cases), using age-standardized rates from the International Agency for Research on Cancer (IARC) via the World Cancer Research Fund (WCRF), based on 2022 estimates, among countries and dependencies.

Rates of cancer

In many developing countries, cancer incidence, insofar as this can be measured, appears much lower, most likely because of the higher death rates due to infectious disease or injury. With the increased control over malaria and tuberculosis in some low- and middle-income countries, incidence of cancer is expected to rise. This is termed an epidemiologic transition in epidemiological terminology. According to WHO estimates as of 2024, the global number of new cancer cases is projected to increase by 77% to 35 million annually by 2050, with the sharpest rises in low Human Development Index countries (142% increase).[2]
