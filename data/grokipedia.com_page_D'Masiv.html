d'Masiv

D'Masiv is an Indonesian rock band based in Jakarta. It consists of 5 members: Rian Ekky Pradipta (vocal), Dwiki Aditya Marsall (guitar), Nurul Damar Ramadan (guitar), Rayyi Kurniawan Iskandar Dinata (bass), and Wahyu Piadji (drums). D'Masiv has been compared with the Indonesian bands Ungu, Nidji, and Peterpan (now known as Noah) because of the popularity of their songs.[1] In 2025, the band ended its contract with Musica Studios and recorded English-language songs in the United States.[2][5][6]

History

D'Masiv was formed on March 3, 2003. D'Masiv's name is derived from the English word "massive" as a hope to be able to achieve the best possible results in the national music scene.[7] Their name began to soar after winning the music competition A Mild Live Wanted in 2007.[7] D'Masiv released their first album titled Perubahan in 2008 with the song "Cinta Ini Membunuhku" as its lead single. The song was so popular that it helped establish their presence in the national music scene.[1] In late June 2008, D'Masiv established a fan club named Masiver.[8] On July 15, 2009, D'Masiv released a mini album containing two songs: "Minta Ampun Aku" and "Jangan Menyerah". According to vocalist Rian, the production of this mini album was very brief and served as a special release to welcome the month of Ramadan, which began on August 22, 2009.[9] The band followed up with their second studio album Perjalanan in December 2009. Throughout the 2010s, D'Masiv continued releasing successful albums, including Persiapan (2012) and the self-titled D'MASIV (2016). Their 2019 album Love featured collaborations and marked further evolution in their sound. In 2022, they experimented with electronica, hip-hop, and dangdut elements on a new release.[10] As of 2025, the band remains active, performing internationally, including at MUSEXPO 2025 in the United States.[5]
