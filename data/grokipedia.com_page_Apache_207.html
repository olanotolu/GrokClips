Apache 207

Volkan Yaman (Turkish pronunciation: [voɫkan jaˈman]; born 23 October 1997), known professionally as Apache 207, is a German rapper and singer of Turkish descent from Ludwigshafen. He became best known for his breakthrough hit "Roller" in August 2019, which was the first of several Top 10 hits. He has since released multiple albums, including "21 Gramm" in 2025.[1]

Life and career

Apache 207 is of Turkish descent, his family having migrated from the Turkish province of Yozgat.[2] He finished high school in 2017 with Abitur at Theodor-Heuss-Gymnasium in Ludwigshafen.[3] The rapper started his mainstream career in June 2018 when he released his debut single "Kleine Hure" without label backing.[4][5] The song was followed up by seven other singles, released over the course of six months.[6] In early 2019, he was signed to musician Baus a's label Two Sides.[7] The first single to be released under the label was "Kein Problem" in April 2019. Initially, the song garnered little attention and did not peak in Germany until late October 2019, at 17. However, the song's music video reached over eight million clicks on YouTube in the following months.[8] In May 2019, he earned his first chart entry in Germany with the release "Brot nach Hause". Even though the single "2 Minuten" climbed up to 35 in Germany, his chart success remained moderate. On 23 August 2019, he released "Roller" which would debut at number two. In the following week, it reached number one on the chart.[9] In Austria and Switzerland, the song peaked within the Top 10.[10] His follow-up releases "200 km/h" and "2002" with Sido became Top 10 hits as well. He achieved his second number one in Germany with the single "Wieso tust du dir das an?" in October 2019.[11] His first EP Platte was released on 25 October 2019.[12] In 2020, Apache 207 released his debut studio album Treppenhaus on 31 July, which topped the German, Austrian, and Swiss album charts.[13] Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, his planned tour was postponed to 2022. His second studio album 2sad2disco followed on 10 December 2021, also reaching number one in Germany.[14] In 2023, he collaborated with Udo Lindenberg on the single "Komet", which became a major hit and earned triple Gold certification in Germany.[15] Later that year, on 9 June, he released his third studio album Gartenstadt, which debuted at number one on the German and Austrian charts.[16] Apache 207 has conducted several arena tours, including the postponed 2022 dates and the Tankeschön Tour in 2024. In 2025, he released the collaborative EP Gesegnet with Luciano on 17 January and his fourth studio album 21 Gramm on 29 August, the latter peaking at number two in Germany.[16] He is scheduled for an extensive arena tour across German-speaking countries starting November 2025.[17]
