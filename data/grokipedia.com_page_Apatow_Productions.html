Apatow Productions

The Apatow Company, doing business as Apatow Productions, is an American film and television production company founded by Judd Apatow in 1999.[1] The company's first television production was the comedy series Freaks and Geeks (1999–2000) and its first film production was the comedy film Anchorman: The Legend of Ron Burgundy (2004). Frequent collaborators include Adam McKay, Will Ferrell, Seth Rogen, and Jason Segel. The current logo consists of a praying mantis.[1]
