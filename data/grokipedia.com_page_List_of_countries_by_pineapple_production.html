List of countries by pineapple production

This is a list of countries by pineapple production from 2016 to 2023, based on data from the Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database.[1] The estimated total world production for pineapples in 2023 was 29,960,000 metric tonnes, an increase from 29,361,138 tonnes in 2022 (up 2.3% from 28,714,479 tonnes in 2021).[1][2] Dependent territories are shown in italics.
