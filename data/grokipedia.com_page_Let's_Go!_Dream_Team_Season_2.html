Let's Go! Dream Team Season 2

Let's Go! Dream Team Season 2 (Korean: 출발 드림팀 시즌2; Hanja: 出發 드림팀 시즌2) was an organized sports variety program that aired on KBS2.[1] The pilot episode aired on September 13, 2009, titled Let's Go Dream Team 2 - Green Team Go!, with regular episodes beginning on October 25, 2009, and the final episode on May 29, 2016. The first season of Dream Team aired from 1999 to 2003. Production director Jeon Jin-hak and MC Lee Chang-myung from season 1 returned for season 2.[2][3]

Slogan

  • Opening (2009–2014): "The legend of ten years! A split second win!" (10년의 신화! 1초의 승부!)
  • Opening (2015–2016): "Hot passion, thrilling victory!" (뜨거운 열정, 짜릿한 승부!)
  • Ending: "To build a society where everyone does his or her best and accepts the results as they are!" (승리를 위해 최선을 다하고 결과에 승복할 줄 아는 사회를 만들기 위해!)[4]

Format

Audience service game

The audience service game was featured from episode 50 to 171. Before the main event, the cast will play an audience service game to show their appreciation of the fans coming out to the event. The members choose a partner, which is usually their manager, but they can also choose a staff member, the team's doctor (2), or an audience. The game is usually played between the Dream Team members but can also be the opposing team. The losing team will pay for the snacks. However, sometimes they continue the game until one member of the losing team is finally chosen to pay. The loser of the game will pay for all the snack expenses.) Audience service game snacks

Wildcard

The wildcard system was used in the relay obstacle races up to episode 169. Before the main event, each team will have a member participate at each obstacle. The winning team with fastest time and fewest eliminations will receive three wildcards, while the other team will receive two wildcards. If both teams have the same number of eliminations, then the team with the fastest time will receive three wildcards.)
