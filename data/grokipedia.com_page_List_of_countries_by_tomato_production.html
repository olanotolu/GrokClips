List of countries by tomato production

This is a list of countries by tomato production from 2016 to 2023, based on data from the Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database.[1] The estimated total world production for tomatoes in 2023 was 192,297,058 metric tonnes, an increase of 3.3% from 186,107,972 tonnes in 2022.[2] China was by far the largest producer, accounting for nearly 36% of global production. Dependent territories are shown in italics.

Production by Country

Note: Data for 2023 is now available from FAOSTAT, with global production of 192,297,059 tonnes.[2]
