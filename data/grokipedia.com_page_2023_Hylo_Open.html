2023 Hylo Open

The 2023 Hylo Open was a badminton tournament which took place at the Saarlandhalle in Saarbrücken, Germany, from 31 October to 5 November 2023[2] and had a total prize of US$210,000.[3][1] The 2023 Hylo Open was the twenty-fifth tournament of the 2023 BWF World Tour. It was a part of the Hylo Open, which had been held since 1988. This tournament was organized by 1. BC Saarbrücken-Bischmisheim with sanction from the BWF.[3]

Venue

This international tournament was held at the Saarlandhalle in Saarbrücken, Germany.[3]
