She has appeared in the main cast or regular recurring cast of a variety of television series, including NCIS: Los Angeles and Masters of Sex, as well as Titans[2] and October Road. She was born in Perth Amboy, New Jersey.[3]

Personal life

Bogush has been married twice. She married Neil Shapiro in January 2000; they divorced in December 2002. She married writer and producer Lukas Reiter in June 2005. Their daughter, Spencer Everly Reiter, was born in August 2013.[3]
