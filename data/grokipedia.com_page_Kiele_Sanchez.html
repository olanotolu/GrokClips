Kiele Sanchez

Kiele Sanchez (/ˈkiːli/ KEE -lee; born October 13, 1977)[1] is an American actress who starred in the A&E Network drama The Glades. Previously, she had starred as Anne Sorelli in The WB comedy-drama Related and as Nikki Fernandez on the main cast in the third season of the ABC television drama series Lost. She also starred in the DirecTV drama series Kingdom on the Audience Network. She portrayed Sydney Voit in Criminal Minds: Evolution (2022–2024).[2]

Early life and education

Sanchez was born in Chicago. Her father, Oscar Sanchez, is a racetrack jockey agent.[3] She is one of four children, and is of Puerto Rican (paternal), French, and Polish (maternal) descent.[4] Sanchez began her acting career at Glenbard North High School in Carol Stream, Illinois, when she performed in a school production of The Grapes of Wrath. Her performance helped her overcome her stage fright, with which she struggled at the time.[4]

Career

In 2000, Sanchez auditioned for MTV's Wanna Be a VJ contest in New York. She was selected as one of the five finalists, but did not win the competition. However, an agent who had seen her on MTV expressed interest in representing her.[5] After her agent recommended a move to Los Angeles, Sanchez auditioned and landed roles in the short-lived drama series That Was Then,[6] and the sitcom Married to the Kellys.[6] In 2003, Sanchez appeared in the film Stuck on You.[6] She joined the cast of the hit ABC show Lost,[6] for the series' third season, as Nikki Fernandez, but due to fan responses to her character, she was killed off six episodes after her first appearance. She had a recurring role on the ABC show Samantha Who?,[6] and played a leading role in the 2008 film Insanitarium,[6] alongside Jesse Metcalfe. She co-starred as Gina in the 2009 thriller A Perfect Getaway.[6] Sanchez starred in the 2010 film 30 Days of Night: Dark Days,[6] taking over the role of Stella Oleson from Melissa George, who played her in the first film. Sanchez also starred in the DirecTV drama series Kingdom which premiered on the Audience Network in the fall of 2014. She also appeared in 49 episodes of The Glades, which ran for four years.[6] In February 2020, she was cast as Lorna Crane, Mickey Haller's second ex-wife and office manager in the CBS series The Lincoln Lawyer, which is based on the 2005 novel of the same name by Michael Connelly.[7] On February 2, 2021, it was announced that Becki Newton would replace Sanchez, after the series moved from CBS to Netflix.[8] In November 2022, Sanchez joined her husband Zach Gilford in a recurring role as Sydney Voit, the wife of Gilford's character Elias Voit in Criminal Minds: Evolution, continuing through 2024.[2] From 2023 to 2024, she portrayed Kate Powell in a recurring role in the final season of Station 19.

Personal life

She was married to screenwriter and director Zach Helm from 2001 to 2008.[9] In 2010, Sanchez began dating actor Zach Gilford, with whom she co-starred in the ABC television pilot The Matadors.[10] The couple became engaged in November 2011,[11] and married on December 29, 2012.[12] The couple also co-starred in The Purge: Anarchy. In August 2015, Sanchez announced that she and Gilford were expecting their first child, a son, that November.[13] In October 2015, the couple announced that they had suffered a late-term miscarriage.[14] In November 2017, the couple had a daughter, Zeppelin Adele, born via surrogate.[15] In 2021, the couple welcomed a son, Revel. Zach Gilford filed for divorce on April 18, 2025.[16][17]
