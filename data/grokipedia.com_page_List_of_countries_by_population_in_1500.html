List of countries by population in 1500

This is a list of countries by population in 1500. The total world population in 1500 has been estimated at around 450 to 540 million.[1] All figures are estimates for the year 1500. The bulk of these numbers are sourced from Alexander V. Avakov's Two Thousand Years of Economic Statistics, Volume 1, pages 12 to 14, which cover population figures from the year 1500 divided into modern borders.[2] Avakov, in turn, cites a variety of sources, mostly Angus Maddison.[3]
