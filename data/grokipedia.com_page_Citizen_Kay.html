Citizen Kay

Citizen Kay (born Kojo Owusu-Ansah, 1994 (age 30–31)) is a Ghanaian-Australian hip hop artist based in Canberra.[1] He migrated to Australia from Koforidua, Ghana, at the age of six. Citizen Kay's EP Demokracy was nominated for Best Urban Album at the ARIA Music Awards of 2015.[2] His debut album, With the People, received the same nomination in 2016.[3] He has since released several albums, including Belly of the Beast (2017) and So, Where Are We? (2023),[4] and earned further recognition, such as nominations at the National Live Music Awards.[5]

Biography

Kojo Owusu-Ansah was born in Ghana and moved to Australia when he was six.[6] He is the older brother of Genesis Owusu.[7] He released his debut single, "YES!" in 2013 and followed it up the next year with an EP called Demokracy, released in November 2014.[8] The EP was nominated for Best Urban Album at the ARIAs and Best Independent Hip-Hop Album at the AIR Awards of 2015. In October 2015 he released his debut album With the People.[9] Citizen Kay released Belly of the Beast in August 2017.[10] In November 2023, he released his third studio album, So, Where Are We?, a self-released project exploring themes of life's cycles.[4]
