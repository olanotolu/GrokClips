2023 Johor Darul Ta'zim F.C. season

The 2023 season is Johor Darul Ta'zim Football Club's 52nd season in club history and 11th season in the Malaysia Super League after rebranding their name from Johor FC. The club achieved a historic domestic quadruple by winning the Super League, FA Cup, Malaysia Cup, and Charity Shield.
