2023 Indiana Hoosiers football team

The 2023 Indiana Hoosiers football team represented Indiana University in the 2023 NCAA Division I FBS football season. The Hoosiers were led by seventh-year head coach Tom Allen. They played their home games at Memorial Stadium in Bloomington, Indiana as members of the Big Ten Conference. Allen was fired at the conclusion of the season. The Indiana Hoosiers football team drew an average home attendance of 44,589 in 2023.

Offseason

Coaching changes

On October 9, 2022, Indiana fired offensive line coach Darren Hiller after six seasons with the program; Hiller had coached the Hoosiers in their appearances in the 2019 Gator Bowl and 2021 Outback Bowl.[1] Following the 2022 season, the Hoosiers hired Wisconsin offensive line coach Bob Bostad to replace Hiller on December 2, 2022; Bostad had coached the Badgers' offensive line during the 2017 Orange Bowl and the 2020 Rose Bowl.[2] On February 17, 2023, head coach Tom Allen hired Matt Guerrieri as co-defensive coordinator and safeties coach; Guerrieri had previously served as a defensive analyst at Ohio State.[3]
