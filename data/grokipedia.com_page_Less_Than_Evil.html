Less Than Evil

Less Than Evil[2] (Korean: 나쁜 형사; RR: Nappeun hyeongsa) is a 2018 South Korean television series based on the British series Luther.[3] It stars Shin Ha-kyun, Lee Seol, Park Ho-san and Kim Gun-woo.[4] It aired on MBC from December 3, 2018 to January 29, 2019.[5][6][7]

Synopsis

Less Than Evil centers on the psychological battle between a brilliant psychopathic murderer and a justice-driven veteran detective.[8] Woo Tae-seok (Shin Ha-kyun) is a lonely, workaholic detective who teeters between good and evil while solving cases. He crosses paths with Eun Sun-jae (Lee Seol), a psychopath and brilliant forensic psychologist with the ability to analyze criminals' minds. The two form an unlikely partnership to investigate crimes, while Tae-seok grapples with his moral dilemmas and a haunting past case.[9]

Cast

Main

  • Shin Ha-kyun as Woo Tae-suk[10] A tough and unscrupulous detective with the highest arrest rate, based on DCI John Luther.
  • Lee Seol as Eun Sun-jae (formerly Bae Yeo-wool) A female psychopath based on antagonist Alice Morgan; revealed to be the adult version of the victim Bae Yeo-wool from 13 years prior.
  • Park Ho-san as Jeon Choon-man A leading detective in the metropolitan area who is at odds with Woo Tae-suk.
  • Kim Gun-woo as Jang Hyung-min A serial killer who pretends to be a prosecutor and Woo Tae-suk's greatest enemy, based on serial killer Tom Marwood.

Supporting

  • Cha Sun-woo as Chae Dong-yoon[11][12] A straight-as-an-arrow detective who graduated early from the police academy, based on Luther's partner DS Justin Ripley.
  • Bae Da-bin[13] as Shin Ga-young A prickly police officer, based on DCI Erin Gray.
  • Bae Yoo-ram[14] as Ban Ji-deuk An expert in IT programming who sets up her own detective agency, based on DS Benny Silver.
  • Yoon Hee-seok[15] as Jo Doo-jin The head of the PR department at the police station and Woo Tae-suk's best friend, based on John Luther's best friend DCI Ian Reed.
  • Yang Ki-won as Lee Moon-ki A policeman on Woo Tae-suk's team.
  • Kim Na-yoon as Team Leader Sung The head of the forensic investigative team, based on Luther's bosses DSU Rose Teller and DSU Martin Schenk.
  • Hong Eun-hee[16] as Kim Hae-joon A lawyer and Woo Tae-suk's wife, based on John Luther's wife Zoe Luther.
  • Cho Yi-hyun[17][18] as young Bae Yeo-wool (in flashbacks) The victim of a case from 13 years ago that Woo Tae-suk cannot forget; later revealed as Eun Sun-jae.
  • Bae Yoon-kyung as Woo Tae-hee Woo Tae-suk's sister.
  • Ryu Tae-ho[14] as Choi Jung-woo A timid and indecisive head detective who works with Jeon Choon-man.
  • Kim Yi-kyung as Kwon Soo-ah A supporting character in select episodes.

Special appearances

  • Ahn Nae-sang as Commissioner general.

Production

The actress Lee Seol was chosen among 300 others who auditioned for the role of Eun Sun-jae to play the female lead. [19] The first script reading was held in early September 2018 with the attendance of cast and crew at MBC in Sangam-dong.[20]
