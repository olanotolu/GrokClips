Lester Cotton

Lester Cotton Sr. (born February 20, 1996) is an American professional football guard who is currently an unrestricted free agent. He was signed as an undrafted free agent following the 2019 NFL draft by the Las Vegas Raiders. He played college football for the Alabama Crimson Tide.[1] Stats at Pro Football Reference

Early life

Lester Cotton was born on February 20, 1996, in Tuscaloosa, Alabama, and went to high school at Central High School in Tuscaloosa. He was a four-star prospect and played in the 2015 Under Armour All-America game.[3] He also was named the no. 3 offensive guard nationally by Rivals.com.[3]

College career

Cotton chose Alabama over scholarship offers from Auburn, USC, Mississippi State and Ole Miss.

2015

He played in four games in his first season.

2016

He started the Crimson Tide's first two games of the season at left guard and started three games more games while playing right guard.[3] He also played in nine other games.[3] He was a blocker for nine 100-yard rushing games.[3] Against USC he played 59 snaps at left guard with and had one knockdown block.[3] He did not allow a pressure or a sack against them as he opened holes for 242 rushing yards.[3] Against Kent State he came off the bench to play 25 snaps and helped the Tide rush for 285 yards.[3] When they played the Tennessee Volunteers Cotton started at right guard and helped cleared a path for 409 yards rushing and 594 yards of offense.[3] Versus Texas A&M he recorded six knockdown blocks and opened holes for 287 yards on the ground and 451 yards of total offense.[3] He played 12 games in 2016.[3]

Las Vegas Raiders

Cotton was signed as an undrafted free agent after the 2019 NFL draft.[5][6] He was released at roster cuts but was later signed to the practice squad.[7] In December he was promoted to the active roster and played one snap in one game.[8][9][10][11] He was active for 3 games but only played in one.[12] He was released at roster cuts in 2020. In February 2021, he was signed again.[13][14] On August 31, 2021, Cotton was waived by the Raiders and re-signed to the practice squad the next day.[15][16] After the Raiders were eliminated in the 2021 Wild Card round of the playoffs, he signed a reserve/future contract on January 17, 2022.[17] Cotton made the Raiders final roster in 2022 as a backup interior lineman. He made his first career start at right guard in Week 2 in a spot start. On December 3, 2022, Cotton was placed on injured reserve.[18] He was released on December 15.

Miami Dolphins

On December 20, 2022, Cotton was signed to the Miami Dolphins practice squad.[19] He signed a reserve/future contract on January 16, 2023.[20] Cotton played in 11 games with Miami in 2024 and was released on November 25, 2024.[21]

New England Patriots

On November 26, 2024, Cotton was claimed off waivers by the [New England Patriots](New England Patriots "New England Patriots").[22] He appeared in three games for the Patriots during the 2024 season.[9] Following the season, Cotton became a restricted free agent, but the Patriots declined to tender a contract in March 2025, making him an unrestricted free agent.[23] As of October 2025, Cotton remains an unsigned free agent.
