2023 FIBA Under-16 Women's Asian Championship

The 2023 FIBA U16 Women's Asian Championship was an international under-16 basketball tournament that was held from 10 to 16 July 2023 in Amman, Jordan.[1] The seventh edition of the biennial competition, this was also the FIBA Asia's qualifying tournament for the 2024 FIBA Under-17 Women's Basketball World Cup in Mexico.[2]

Qualified Teams

Qualified teams

For Division A:[3]
  • Semifinalists of the 2022 FIBA Under-16 Women's Asian Championship:
    • Australia
    • Japan
    • South Korea
    • New Zealand
  • Rest of top seven teams from the 2017 FIBA Under-16 Women's Asian Championship:
    • China
    • Chinese Taipei
    • Thailand
  • Division B winners and runner-ups at the 2022 FIBA Under-16 Women's Asian Championship:
    • Samoa
    • Syria
For Division B:[4]
  • The host nation:
    • Jordan
  • 4th placer of the 2022 FIBA Under-15 Women's Oceania Championship:
    • Guam
  • Teams from FIBA Asia on a first-come first-registered basis:
    • Hong Kong
    • Iran
    • Malaysia
    • Maldives
    • Philippines
    • Singapore
Included were the FIBA World Rankings prior to the tournament for all participating teams in each division.[5]
