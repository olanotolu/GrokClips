2023 FIBA 3x3 World Cup – Men's tournament

The 2023 FIBA 3x3 World Cup was held in Vienna, Austria from 30 May to 4 June 2023, and contested by 20 teams. Serbia won their second consecutive and sixth title overall with a win over the United States, with Latvia winning bronze.

Qualified teams

Twenty teams qualified for the tournament through the host nation spot, performances in the continental championships, and the FIBA 3x3 World Cup Qualifier. All five FIBA zones were represented.
