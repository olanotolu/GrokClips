Ding Yuxi

In this Chinese name, the family name is Ding. Ding Yuxi (Simplified Chinese: 丁禹兮; born 20 July 1995), also known as Ryan Ding, is a Chinese actor. He gained recognition for his role as Han Shuo in The Romance of Tiger and Rose (2020). He is also known for his roles in Intense Love (2020), Moonlight (2021), Love You Seven Times (2023), and Love Game in Eastern Fantasy (2024).

Ding Yuxi's Career

Beginnings

In 2013, Ding Yuxi was admitted to the Shanghai Film Art Academy, majoring in Theatre, Film and Television Acting.[1] Later, he entered the College of Continuing Education of Shanghai Theatre Academy, majoring in Directing.[2] In 2017, he made his debut by starring in the movie, The Game of Asura. He played the young and beautiful, fashion-loving heroine Zhou Yiyi's boyfriend Cha Jian.[3]

Rising popularity

In 2018, Ding gained attention for his supporting role of Dongfang Bubai in the drama New Smiling Proud Wanderer.[4][5] During the same year, he participated in the variety show, PhantaCity, where guests perform an immersive performance stage, with music performance as the main form.[6] Later that year he also participated in I Actor, a variety show where rookie actors and actresses improve their acting skills along with strengthening their morals. The strongest 8 will be chosen at the end and have a drama specially designed for them. Ding eventually won first place.[7] In 2019, Ding starred in the web drama, Eight Minutes of Warmth.[8] He later on starred as the male lead in the drama, The Future's Secret, the drama starring the 8 final winners of I Actor.[9] In 2020, Ding starred in the romance drama Intense Love as a doctor.[10] He then starred in historical romance drama The Romance of Tiger and Rose with Zhao Lusi.[11] The series was a hit, and was praised for its interesting setup and plot.[12] Ding started gaining increased popularity for starring in these two dramas, receiving the title of "May Boyfriend".[13][14] In May 2021, Ding starred in the romantic comedy drama Moonlight alongside Yu Shuxin.[15] In 2022, he reunited with Zhao Lusi in the fantasy drama Prodigy Healer.[16] In 2023, Ding starred in Dreaming of a Spring Boudoir with Peng Xiaoran[17] and the xianxia drama Love You Seven Times with Kido Yu.[18] In 2024, he led the historical mystery drama Dali Temple Log.[19] In November 2024, Ding Yuxi reunited with co-star Yu Shuxin in Love Game in Eastern Fantasy based on the novel Black Lotus Strategy Guide premiering on Tencent Video and WeTV. The series received a Douban rating of 7.4 out of 10 as of October 2025, one of the highest-rated Chinese fantasy series of 2024.[20][21] It also received high ratings on international streaming platforms such as Rakuten Viki with a rating of 8.9 as of October 2025.[22] Due to his skyrocketing popularity from this role, he performed a cover of "Ning Mou (凝眸)" (originally from the Love Game in Eastern Fantasy OST), on the music competition show The Next Singer.[23] The cast reunited for a 3-episode variety show, Star Journey: Weizhou Island, embarking on a 3-day, 2-night trip to Weizhou Island.[24] In late 2024, Ding starred in the crime drama Black and White Forest with Ning Li.[25] In 2025, he appeared in Huai Shui Zhu Ting.[26]
