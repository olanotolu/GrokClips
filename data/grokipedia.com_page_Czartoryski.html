Rivalry with Other Magnate Families

The Czartoryski family's ascendancy in the mid-18th century involved sharp rivalries with other magnate houses, notably the Potocki, as factions maneuvered for control over royal elections, Sejm proceedings, and influence networks within the Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth. Controversies and Critical Assessments
