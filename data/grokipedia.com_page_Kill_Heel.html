Kill Heel

Kill Heel (Korean: 킬힐; RR: Kilhil) is a 2022 South Korean television series directed by Noh Do-cheol and starring Kim Ha-neul, Lee Hye-young and Kim Sung-ryung. The series depicts the lives of three women, who have strong desire to achieve success in the home shopping industry.[3] It premiered on tvN on March 9, 2022, and aired every Wednesday and Thursday at 22:30 (KST) for 14 episodes.[4]

Cast and characters

Main

  • Kim Ha-neul as Woo-hyun[5] 39 years old, a show host at UNI Home Shopping. Once a very successful show host with a lot of accordance, now her career is on decline to the level of selling toilet paper.
  • Lee Hye-young as Gi Mo-ran[6]
    • Kim Min-joo as young Gi Mo-ran[7]
    CFO, UNI Home Shopping
  • Kim Sung-ryung as Bae Ok-sun[8]
    • Min Soo-hwa as young Bae Ok-sun[9]
    52 years old, a high-class and UNI home shopping signboard show host.

Supporting

People around Woo Hyun

  • Kim Jin-woo as Kim Do-il, Woo Hyun's jobless husband[10]
  • Jung Seo-yeon as Kim Ji-yoon, Woo Hyun and Do-il's only daughter[11]
  • Jeon Guk-hyang as Do-il's mother (Woo Hyun's mother-in-law)[12]

People around Bae Ok-sun

  • Jeon No-min as Choi In-guk[13] In mid 50s, Bae Ok-sun's husband and the son of a prominent politician family. He is currently in the process of campaigning for parliamentary elections.
  • Yoon Hyun-soo as Choi Jeong-hyeon[14] Bae Ok-sun's son.

UNI Home Shopping People

  • Kim Jae-chul as Hyun-wook[15] In late 40s, the eldest son of UNI Group and president of UNI Home Shopping who has a polite and courteous personality.
  • Jung Eui-jae as Seo Jun-beom[16] In mid 30s, a PD at UNI Home Shopping.
  • Moon Ji-in as Noh Seong-woo[17] A new PD of home shopping.
  • Shim Wan-joon as Park Jong-soo[18] The team leader of a home shopping company.
  • Shin Joo-ah as Go Eun-nara[19] A UNI home shopping show host
  • Kim Do-yeon as Kim Soo-wan[20] The head of hair and makeup in the dressing room in home shopping.
  • Kim Hyo-sun as Ahn An-na[21] UNI Home Shopping Fashion Team Product Planning, who is a popular MD and self-centered to own what she wants.
  • Park So-eun as Im In-jin[22] The PD of home shopping.
  • Yoo Jang-young as Shim Sang-chan[23] A shopping host with 2-3 years of experience, he is a savvy person who broadcasts almost all products in home shopping.
  • Park Hee-jin as Noh Da-bi[24] A fashion person who once worked as a stylist, but after failing to appear on a fashion show once, she never had another chance to appear on a fashion show again. She is currently established in the food and daily necessities field.

Others

  • Han Soo-yeon as Ham Shin-ae[25] Hyun-wook's wife and the daughter of a famous chaebol family.
  • Kim Hyun-wook as James Miller, Gi Mo-ran's son, a young man in his twenties[26]

Special appearance

  • Jung A-mi as Ham Shin-ae's mother (ep. 11)[27]
  • Jung Won-kwan as Delivery man[28]

Production

On November 3, 2021, it was reported that Kim Ha-neul is considering to appear in the TV series a year after 18 Again.[29] Lee Hye-young is appearing in TV series after 3 years, her last appearance was in tvN's 2018 TV series Lawless Lawyer.[30] Park Eun-seok was first offered the role of Seo Jun-beom, a PD in home shopping company, which later went to Jung Eui-jae.[31] On January 14, 2022, the script reading site was revealed by releasing photos.[32] The series' premiere was originally scheduled for February 23, 2022, but was delayed by two weeks to March 9, 2022, after a staff member tested positive for COVID-19.[33] Originally planned for 16 episodes, the series was shortened to 14 episodes due to scheduling strategy changes.[34]

Release

Kill Heel was originally scheduled to be released on February 23, 2022. However, the filming schedule changed due to the aftermath of COVID-19 confirmed at the filming site and the premiere date was postponed for two weeks to March 9, 2022.[33] Kill Heel was originally organized as 16 episodes, ended with 14 episodes, due to internal organizational strategic reasons.[34]
