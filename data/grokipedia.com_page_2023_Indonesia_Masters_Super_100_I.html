2023 Indonesia Masters Super 100 I

The 2023 Indonesia Masters Super 100 I (officially known as the BNI Indonesia Masters I 2023 for sponsorship reasons) was a badminton tournament which took place at GOR PBSI Pancing in Deli Serdang, Indonesia, from 5 to 10 September 2023 and had a total purse of $100,000.[1][2] The 2023 Indonesia Masters Super 100 I was the twenty-second tournament of the 2023 BWF World Tour and also part of the Indonesia Masters Super 100 championships, which had been held since 2018. This tournament was organized by the Badminton Association of Indonesia and sanctioned by the BWF.[1]

Venue

This tournament was held at GOR PBSI Pancing in Deli Serdang, North Sumatra, Indonesia.[1]
