Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike discography

Belgian DJ and record producer duo Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike have released multiple compilation albums, starting with their first, Smash The House!, in 2010. Their discography includes numerous singles, extended plays, and remixes, with key early hits like "Mammoth" (2013, with Moguai) charting in multiple countries including Belgium and the Netherlands. "Tremor" (2014, with Martin Garrix) peaked in five countries as of 2014. As of 2025, they continue releasing music, including their debut studio album announced in 2023 and recent singles like "Heroine" and collaborations under 3 Are Legend.[1][2]

Albums

No studio albums have been released as of October 2025. Their debut studio album, titled Rewind + Repeat, was announced in July 2023 but remains unreleased.
