Max George

Maximillian Albert George[1] (born 6 September 1988) is an English singer and actor, best known as a lead singer of the boy band The Wanted, with whom he reunited in 2024.web:70 George started his career as a footballer, playing for Preston North End. After an injury ended his football career, he decided to pursue a music career. George made his singing debut with the band Avenue. After the group disbanded in 2009, George joined the newly formed band, The Wanted. In 2014, following The Wanted's success in the United States, he landed his first acting role in the sixth season of the American television series Glee in the role of Clint, the leader of "Vocal Adrenaline".[2] In 2013, George starred in the E! channel reality series The Wanted Life.[3] In late 2024 and early 2025, George underwent heart surgery for a 2:1 heart block, including pacemaker implantation, but recovered and returned to performing by October 2025.[4][5]

Early life

George was born on 6 September 1988 in Swinton, Greater Manchester, and studied at Bolton School.[6] He is the youngest son of Barbara and Mark George, with an older brother named Jack.[7] He was raised in Manchester.[8] Before music, he was a footballer with Preston North End and was on the verge of signing a two-year deal with them.[8] He has been a Manchester City F.C. supporter since a young age.[9]

Music career

Avenue and The X Factor

In 2005 George joined the five-piece boy band Avenue made up of himself, Jonathan Lloyd, Scott Clarke, Ross Candy and Jamie Tinkler. After applying to The X Factor in 2006, the band auditioned for the third series of the competition singing an a cappella version of the Will Young song, "Leave Right Now".[10] They were mentored by Louis Walsh after completing the bootcamp and judges' house portion. Making it to the top 12 contestants, the band then signed a management contract with the series. After it was discovered they were already under contract with another management firm, they were disqualified from the show.[11][12][13] Following the disqualification, they were signed to the Crown Music Management recording label and were awarded an album deal with Island Records. With Andy Brown joining as a member of Avenue replacing Jamie Tinkler, the band went on to have some success beyond their X Factor appearances. In 2008, Avenue released their debut single, "Last Goodbye", which reached number 50 on the UK Singles Chart. Their plans to go on tour were cancelled. They had a follow-up release with "Can You Feel It?." The group disbanded in 2009.[14]

The Wanted

In 2009, a mass audition was held by Jayne Collins to form a boy band, after successfully launching Parade and The Saturdays. George auditioned and was selected as one of five members, along with Nathan Sykes, Siva Kaneswaran, Tom Parker and Jay McGuiness out of the thousands of others who auditioned. The band was formed and together they worked on their debut album before finding a perfect name for their band, The Wanted. Their debut single "All Time Low" was released on 25 July 2010, debuted at number one on the UK Singles Chart. Their second single, "Heart Vacancy" was released on 17 October 2010. The song hit number two on the UK Singles Chart, and at number 18 in Ireland. Their third single, "Lose My Mind" was released on 26 December 2010. The song peaked number 19 on the UK Singles Chart, and peaked at number 30 in Ireland. In 2011, the single was released in Germany and in 2012, in the United States.[15] In 2013, in support of a crossover appeal to the American music market, the group starred in their own reality series on E!.[16] The series, The Wanted Life only aired for one season. The band announced their decision to break in January 2014.[17] As a member of the band, George contributed to over 12 million records sold worldwide.

Solo artist

In January 2014, George revealed in an interview that he was signed as a solo artist by Scooter Braun.[18] On 3 July 2018 he released his first solo single "Barcelona". The song has over 6.8 million streams on Spotify (as of October 2025).[19] On 24 May 2019 he released his second single "Better On Me".[20] He followed this with "Til I Met You" on 13 September 2019 and "That's Not Me" on 24 January 2020. Additional singles include "Give Yourself to Me" in 2020.

Other ventures

Modelling

In June 2013, George was named as the new face and spokesmodel for Buffalo denim's fall line. He starred in the campaign opposite Sports Illustrated swimsuit model, Hannah Davis.[21] In November 2013, he did an underwear campaign for Buffalo again.[22]

Acting career

George is signed with Creative Artists Agency since 2013.[23] In June 2014, George revealed his decision to enroll in elocution classes, to help improve his pursuit of an acting career.[24][25] In September 2014, it was confirmed that he had joined the cast of Glee (TV series) in the role of Clint, a bully and the lead vocalist of Vocal Adrenaline, after Mark Salling shared a photo of him on Twitter hanging out with Max on the set of the final season of the US comedy drama.[26][27][28] He made his first on screen appearance on 9 January 2015. In November 2023, George was cast in the UK tour of Kay Mellor's The Syndicate, marking his stage acting debut, but he withdrew in March 2024 due to personal reasons.[29][30] In April 2024, George was cast as Parson Nathaniel in Jeff Wayne's Musical Version of The War of the Worlds for the 2025 The Spirit of Man tour.[31] George performed the role during the 2025 tour, returning to work after undergoing heart surgery in late 2024.[32]

Reality television

On 2 February 2015, it was announced that George had joined the cast of Bear Grylls' reality television survival series, Bear Grylls: Mission Survive.[33] On 1 September 2020, it was announced that George would be taking part in the eighteenth series of Strictly Come Dancing.[34] He was partnered with Australian dancer Dianne Buswell. They were eliminated on week 4 of the competition, finishing in ninth place. In 2022, he competed in the ITV series The Games (British TV series) .[35] In 2023, he took part in Scared of the Dark (TV series) on Channel 4.

Radio

On 7 July 2021, it was announced that George would be joining Hits Radio, presenting Friday Night Hits every Friday from 7-10pm across the Hits Radio Network. His first show was on 9 July 2021. He presented the show regularly until around 2023, and as of 2025, continued to appear on Hits Radio occasionally.[36][37]

Personal life

George was in a relationship with actress Michelle Keegan from December 2010.[38] The couple became engaged in June 2011, but ended their relationship the following year.[39] In October 2013, George confirmed in an interview that he was dating Danish swimwear model Nina Agdal.[40] They split in February 2014.[41] He was rumored to be dating beauty queen Amy Willerton in 2014.[42] In October 2014, he confirmed being in a relationship with Miss Oklahoma contestant Carrie Baker.[43][44] In 2018, he began dating Stacey Giggs, but they split in August 2022 after he was embroiled in cheating rumours with co-star Maisie Smith.[45] In September 2022, it was revealed that he was dating EastEnders actress Maisie Smith. As of October 2025, the relationship is ongoing.[46] In December 2024, George was unexpectedly rushed into hospital after he felt unwell. On 18 December, he underwent heart-block surgery and was fitted with a pacemaker.[4][5] In January 2025, he underwent a second heart surgery.[47] In September 2025, he was hospitalized again due to ongoing health issues.[48]
