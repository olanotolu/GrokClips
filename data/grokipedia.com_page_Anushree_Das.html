Anushree Das

Anushree Das is an Indian actress of Bengali cinema and television.[4] Her first acting assignment was Shosti Theke Shosti, a jatra that starred Supriya Devi in the lead.[5] She made her silver screen debut in Bhabesh Kundu's Bengali film Bourani (1991).[6]

Career

She started playing lead actress roles in the films Bourani directed by Bhabesh Kundu in 1991 and as the lead character Rupban in Rupban Kanya in 1992. She appeared in Bhai Amar Bhai (1996) and Praner Cheye Priyo (1998). Then she started her career as a television actress with Zee Bangla's Khela and is mostly seen in serials of Magic Moments Motion Pictures. She has continued appearing in Bengali television serials, including Meyebela (2023), Dwitiyo Basanta (2023â€“2024), and initially in Chirodini Tumi Je Amar (2025), produced by various channels like Star Jalsha and Zee Bangla.[7][2][8]
