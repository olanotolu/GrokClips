List of current NCAA Division I women's basketball coaches

As of the 2025–26 season, 363 women's college basketball programs compete in NCAA Division I, including full D-I members and programs transitioning from Division II. Four schools (Bellarmine, Tarleton, UC San Diego, and Utah Tech) completed transitions from Division II at the end of the 2023–24 school year. At the same time, Mercyhurst and West Georgia began their transitions from Division II, with additional schools in process as of 2025. Each program employs a head coach. As of the 2025–26 college season, the longest-tenured head coach at their current school is Geno Auriemma of UConn, who has held that position since 1985–86. Conference affiliations reflect those in place for the 2025–26 season. This list also reflects coaching vacancies announced at the end of the 2024–25 season.
