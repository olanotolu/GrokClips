Leslie Cheung discography

This is the discography of Hong Kong singer Leslie Cheung. Throughout his career, he released over 40 music albums.[1] His studio album, Most Beloved (1995), sold over 300,000 copies in Hong Kong[2] and over 500,000 copies in South Korea,[] and remains the best-selling album by a Hong Kong singer in South Korea.[] In addition, Admiration (1987) sold over 300,000 copies in South Korea.[]
