List of current NBA Eastern Conference team rosters

Below is a list of current National Basketball Association (NBA) Eastern Conference team rosters for the 2025–26 season, as of October 2025. NBA rosters are limited to 15 players during the regular season, plus three players on two-way contracts, expanding each team's roster to 18 players maximum. Teams may carry up to 21 players during the offseason.[1] There are 15 teams in the Eastern Conference.
