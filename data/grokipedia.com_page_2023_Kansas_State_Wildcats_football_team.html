Rankings from AP Poll The 2023 Kansas State Wildcats football team represented Kansas State University in the Big 12 Conference during the 2023 NCAA Division I FBS football season. The Wildcats were led by Chris Klieman in his fifth year as their head coach. They finished the season with a 9–4 overall record and 6–3 mark in Big 12 play, earning a berth in the Pop-Tarts Bowl, where they defeated NC State 28–19 to claim the bowl championship. They played their home games at Bill Snyder Family Football Stadium in Manhattan, Kansas. The Kansas State Wildcats football team drew an average home attendance of 51,957 in 2023.[1]
