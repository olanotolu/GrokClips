List of countries by population in 1700

This is a list of countries by population in 1700. Estimate numbers are from the beginning of the year and exact population figures are for countries that held a census on various dates in the 1700s. The figures are primarily historical polity totals, with some approximations using modern borders from Alexander V. Avakov's Two Thousand Years of Economic Statistics, Volume 1, pages 18 to 20; Avakov cites various sources, including Angus Maddison. Other entries use specific historical sources.
